Why So Many Of Billie Eilish's Outfits Have Caused Scandals

As early as 13 years old, Billie Eilish has grown into the adult that she is in the limelight. With her natural musical composition and producing talent, the 20-year-old artist has proven she deserves all the recognition and fan base that she has now. However, great fame comes with great responsibility—one of which is how she dresses.

Why has Billie Eilish attracted more attention now because of her outfits? Is she going through a new phase of discovering herself, or is the media only making a big fuss about it? Keep reading to find out...

6 Billie Eilish's Clothing Style

Billie Eilish is a complete sneakerhead; alongside this is her love for baggy, street clothing that makes up her style. As Vogue calls her, she has been the ultimate expression of Gen Z's eclectic approach to fashion. Many of her fans in the same age group even treat Billie as a fashion icon, duping her outfits and style.

In her teenage years, she has been known to wear laid-back, oversized shirts and baggy cargo pants, typically in black, brown, and bright-colored clothing. Her baggy outfits became her trademark, matching her bright green hair from 2019 to 2020.

In 2020, she explored more colorful outfit combinations, primarily matching neon green accents with her hair. But in 2021, she had a surprising transformation from her all-colorful style to a blonde, neutral-fashioned Eilish.

5 Billie Eilish's Met Gala Outfit In 2021

The Met Gala host surprised the public with her all-blonde hair and glamorous Hollywood-inspired dress. The media and fans correlated Billie Eilish as a Holiday Barbie. Styled by Alessandro Michele, Gucci's creative director, Billie Eilish wowed fashion enthusiasts because her Monroe-inspired ballgown is made from recycled materials.

Wearing an outfit utterly different from her usual baggy clothes, Billie Eilish proved she could strut down the red carpet with less punk and more finesse. However, despite her classy appearance, she was still not free from critiques and shaming for the tight corset. Others would tell her dress was too revealing, questioning if Billie Eilish was truly comfortable wearing such an outfit.

4 Where Does Billie Eilish Get Her Clothes From?

Despite the simple-looking outfits, Billie Eilish pulls off, high-end fashion brands such as Gucci, MCM, and Louis Vuitton love dressing her.

As Alessandro Michele designed her 2021 Met Gala dress, it seems that Gucci has a special place in her heart to trust them with what she wears. She has also been regularly wearing other Gucci items such as matching tops and bottoms in other red carpet events.

In 2019, Billie Eilish also made her style more accessible to her fans by collaborating with Freak City to create a clothing line inspired by street fashion. As with her trademark neon green hair era, Freak City sold tank tops, shorts, and tube tops with Graffiti designs in neon green and white colors.

According to Nylon, an essential to a Billie Eilish-inspired outfit is a vest that she gets from Louis Vuitton. Fans can also buy her iconic goth-like necklace through another collaboration she has with Heart of Bone. She also has other brand promotions with Burberry through public appearances where she is spotted wearing one of their clothes.

3 Why Has Billie Eilish Stopped Wearing Baggy Clothes?

In 2022, she has now been more open to discussing her struggles in dealing with her personal life, career, and body image, which he rarely did in the past few years. Billie Eilish has not only struggled with people shaming her baggy outfits but also with her disability. Billie even tells David Letterman that her Tourette's Syndrome has been a topic to laugh about by the public.

Another topic she has recently been open about is the impact of years of being in the limelight on her perception of her body image. She tells Vogue that she used to love wearing dresses as a child, but because of the criticisms from adults who found malice in her curvy body, she stopped wearing them.

However, after being the cover story of Vogue 2021, where she debuted as the blonde Billie wearing a corset and classic lingerie, she found confidence in stepping away from baggy clothes. Billie says she has learned not to mind what people tell about her body, saying that she should cover it up and go back to her love of wearing dresses and exploring her style even more.

2 Does Billie Eilish Have Body Dysmorphia?

With the media surrounding her every move and outfit since the release of her first-ever global hit, Ocean eyes, she has grown to have mixed feelings about being a fashion icon to the Gen Zs. The attention had adverse effects on her, such as depression and body dysmorphia.

Even when she would lose weight, as what the media would want from her, she still saw herself as more significant than her actual size. It also didn't help that her anxiety added another layer to the struggle for her to accept her curvy body shape.

1 Billie Eilish Experienced Body-Shaming

As early as 17 years old, the young Billie Eilish has struggled to feel comfortable in her body because of all the body-shaming she received from the public. It resulted in her resorting to baggy clothes to hide her body shape. In 2021, there was even a viral picture of Billie Eilish being spotted wearing a tank top which is her unusual outfit, resulting in the media calling it one of Billie's scandals.
