The Truth About Brandon Thomas Lee's Girlfriend And His Past Relationships

Throughout Hollywood history, there have been a handful of celebrity couples who’ve truly managed to capture the world’s attention. For example, after a private recording of Pamela Anderson and Tommy Lee was leaked, it seemed like talk about the power couple was everywhere for a while. In fact, that time in the couple’s life is so memorable more than two decades later that it inspired the recent biographical miniseries Pan & Tommy.

Given how intensely the press and public paid attention to Pamela Anderson and Tommy Lee at one point, their eldest son Brandon must have some idea of what that was like. With that in mind, you’d think that Brandon might avoid the spotlight but instead, he joined the cast of The Hills: New Beginnings. While some people question how fake The Hills: New Beginnings might be, the show made fans interested in Brandon’s personal life. As a result, there are a lot of people who want to know about who Brandon is involved with now and who he dated in the past.

Who Is Brandon Thomas Lee Dating Now?

Ever since Brandon Thomas Lee became well known in his own right, it has seemed like he has a complicated relationship with fame. After all, at times Brandon seems to desire notoriety a great deal but at the same time, he also seems like he wants people to believe that he doesn’t care about the spotlight. Given the way that he seems to address his fame, it shouldn’t surprise anyone that even though it seems clear that Brandon has a girlfriend, he hasn’t confirmed the relationship to date.

Since October of 2021, it has seemed like Brandon Thomas Lee has been involved with an Australian influencer name Lily Easton. After all, in that month both of them were on vacation in Mexico and they tagged one another in their Instagram stories. Of course, the two young people easily could have been nothing but friends at that time.

The following month in November of 2021, it became a lot easier to conclude that Brandon Thomas Lee and Lily Easton were a couple. On November 4th, Brandon held an event for his clothing brand that had many of his friends and family in attendance. At the event, Brandon posed with Lily by his side for a photo with his rich and famous parents and his brother along with their partners.

Since November of 2021, Brandon Thomas Lee and Lily Easton’s social media activity has provided more proof that they are a couple. For example, they’ve commented on each other’s posts and when Lily added a photo of a shirtless Brandon bringing her a bowl of cereal, she included a caption that read “Every morning”.

Brandon Thomas Lee’s Romantic History

Years before fans came to believe that he was dating Lily Easton, headlines about Brandon Thomas Lee’s love life began to appear online. For example, all the way back in 2015, reports that Brandon was dating Bella Thorne began appearing online. Of course, since it wasn’t until years later that The Hills: New Beginning premiered on television, Brandon was simply known as Tommy Lee and Pamela Anderson’s son at that time.

Since Brandon Thomas Lee and Bella Thorne’s relationship was very short-lived, that freed him up to begin dating Raina Lawson later in 2015. A fairly successful model who has worked with some notable brands, Raina also is passionate about her love for animals. In an awkward twist, it turns out that Raina was friends with Brandon’s ex Bella Thorne.

Once Brandon Thomas Lee became single again, he next went on to get involved with Pyper Smith in 2016 and they stayed together for roughly a year. Just like Raina Lawson, Smith is a model and in 2020 Brandon got involved with another woman who gets paid to pose for photos, Daniella Beckerman. Signed to Elite Model Management in LA and Miami, Beckerman also is a social media influencer and she appeared in episodes of The Hills: New Beginnings on Brandon’s shoulder.

Of all the women that Brandon Thomas Lee has dated, the most famous by far is the actor Kathryn Newton who he was involved with from August to October of 2019.

After first becoming famous when she joined the cast of the sitcom Gary Unmarried, Newton went on to star in Big Little Lies which allowed her to work with some legendary stars. On the big screen, Newton has starred in movies like Paranormal Activity 4, Blockers, Detective Pikachu, and Freaky among others.
