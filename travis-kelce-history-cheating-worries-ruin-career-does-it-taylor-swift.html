Travis Kelce Has A History Of Cheating, But Worries Hell Ruin His Career If He Does It To Taylor

Highlights

  • Travis Kelce is reportedly worried about potentially damaging his career if he does anything to hurt Taylor Swift due to the backlash from her fan base, the Swifties.
  • Body language expert Judi James suggests that Travis appeared nervous and on edge during a recent public appearance with Taylor, possibly due to the pressure of being in the public eye.
  • Travis' ex-girlfriend has warned Taylor about his past infidelity, adding to the concerns surrounding their relationship.

Travis Kelce has been accused of cheating in a relationship before, with one of his ex-girlfriends even issuing Taylor Swift a direct warning over his past behavior. Nevertheless, Travis and Taylor appear to be going strong.

But the NFL star reportedly has a growing concern – that he could permanently damage his career if he does something to hurt Taylor, thanks to the wrath of her fan base, affectionately known as Swifties.

Travis’ Body Language Suggests He’s Nervous In Front Of Swifties

Speaking to Metro, Judi James, a body language expert, said that while Taylor seemed all over Travis during a recent appearance at her Buenos Aires, Argentina Eras tour, he seemed a bit on edge by her display of love, which could be a sign of his nervousness in the public eye.

"Most guys going into a new relationship will get the "treat her right, or you'll have me to answer to" lecture from a girl's dad, but Travis must know he's also got that formidable army of Swifties to answer to, too, if he cools or cheats,” James explained, revealing the additional pressure on Travis.

“The body language and the choreography of this kiss made it look like the climax of a movie like The Bodyguard , with Taylor sprinting off stage to throw herself into the arms of her man while the fans and the audience go wild with approval,” the expert continued.

"There seemed to be no doubt that this was Taylor's open declaration of love for Kelce, but the football star didn't quite seem to have got the memo when it came to sweeping her up in his arms a la Kevin Costner and carrying her off into the Green Room,” James noted.

There have also been reports that Travis’ family is worried about the level of attention his new relationship is receiving, as it’s becoming a security concern since the NFL star isn’t used to this level of publicity (or hard-core fans).

Travis’ Ex Warned Taylor That He’s A Cheater

However, Taylor also has reason for concern, as Travis’ ex-girlfriend Maya Benberry directly spoke to the singer in a new interview with Daily Mail. She not only called the athlete out for cheating on her, but suggested that Taylor should be cautious of his overall intentions.

Travis has also been called out for past comments, including a podcast episode where he claimed to want a “breeder” to have children with, only to give his mother more grandchildren.

One report claimed that Taylor’s loved ones are concerned about their fast-paced relationship. Selena Gomez allegedly even spoke to the singer about her worries. However, others in Taylor’s inner circle have defended her new romance, like Gigi Hadid, who recently commented on a Perez Hilton article that claimed she disapproved of Taylor and Travis together, only to deny the alleged anonymous source used in the story.

For now, Taylor and Travis haven’t directly commented on the controversy surrounding their relationship.
