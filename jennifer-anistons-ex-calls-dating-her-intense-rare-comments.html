Jennifer Anistons Ex Calls Dating Her Intense In Rare Comments About Their Relationship

Highlights

  • Jennifer Aniston's ex opens up about intense relationship with the star, highlighting struggles with paparazzi invasion.

  • Celebrities like Prince Harry and Britney Spears have found success with memoirs, possibly influencing Aniston's decision to share her story.

Jennifer Aniston has dated many famous men over the years, and despite speaking out about how hurtful media speculation can be, tons of focus is still given to her personal life. Recently, one of the actress’ past boyfriends opened up about what dating the A-list celebrity was like.

Why Paul Sculfor Says Dating Jennifer Aniston Was “Intense”

Model Paul Sculfor, who also dated Cameron Diaz, recently opened up about his relationships with high-profile women to the Sunday Times. Paul admitted it was hard dealing with the paparazzi, saying he had very little privacy at some points.

“It got intense, silly,” he revealed. “I'd be taking the bins out in an old tracksuit and there'd be a guy with a camera in my face? Come on!"

Jen hasn’t commented on her ex’s recent interview, but the notoriously private celeb is known to keep mentions of her private life to a limit. But that may be changing given recent reports Aniston is thinking of writing a memoir.

Jennifer May Open Up About One Of Her Exes Soon

Sources claim Aniston is thinking of writing a book with an emphasis on the dissolution of her marriage to Brad Pitt. It came in 2005 after her met Angelina Jolie while flming Mr. and Mrs. Smith.

It’s largely believed Angelina and Brad had an affair, given they started dating so quickly after the divorce news was made public (and Angelina had a reputation for going for married men). Though they married and had six kids, Angelina and Brad have since divorced, but remain locked in a nasty custody battle.

“Jen knows people want to hear all about her love affair with Brad and the truth about their divorce, especially since Brad and Angelina have now split and Jen and Brad have reconnected,” a source alleged to Life & Style.

“Jen has had a lot of therapy to make peace with her failed marriage to Brad and other romances that didn’t work out,” they went on. “But there is so much more to the story, especially about why they never had a child together.”

With some celebrities making multi millions from a memoir, the potential paycheck could be a big draw for Aniston (though with a $320 million net worth, she’s not hurting for cash). Still, a memoir would be a chance to share her story while making bank from it.

According to reports, Prince Harry secured a substantial $20 million advance for his memoir, which hit the shelves last year, with the potential for additional earnings from sales. Similarly, Britney Spears is believed to have garnered a minimum of $12.5 million for her memoir, The Woman in Me.

Given Britney's expressed interest in penning a second book, it appears to be a profitable avenue for celebrities, a path Aniston may soon be venturing on, too.
