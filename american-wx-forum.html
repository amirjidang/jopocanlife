Interesting New England Weather Facts

  • New England is known for its variable weather conditions, with rapid shifts from sunny to stormy weather.
  • The region experiences distinct seasons, with hot summers and cold, snowy winters.
  • Nor’easters, powerful winter storms that bring heavy snowfall and strong winds, are a common weather occurrence in New England.
  • Coastal areas in New England are prone to the effects of tropical storms and hurricanes during the Atlantic hurricane season.
  • New England’s geography, including its mountains and coastline, contributes to its diverse microclimates and localized weather patterns.
