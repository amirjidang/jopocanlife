Details Of The Mansion

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck just recently bought 10-bedroom estate worth $50 million. The couple who rekindled their relationship last year have decided to move in together and agreed on a deal to buy a 20,000 sq ft mansion located on the Bel Air region of Los Angeles. The estate that the couple bought has plenty of space for the three children of the former Batman actor and the twins of the Jenny from The Block singer. It was reported that the two are keen for all of them to stay together in one house.

The property was initially listed up for grabs in February by the owner, billionaire Texas Todd Lemkin, for $65 million but the couple successfully closed the sale for $50 million. The property was listed by agents Linda May and Drew Fenton under Hilton and Hyland. It was reported that Todd Lemkin acquired the Bel-Air estate for around $27.6 million in 2016 and six years later, he sold it for almost double of the original price. However it was believed that Lemkin has spent millions on the renovation of the estate to update the aesthetics of the property and increase its value.

The couple is not the only celebrities who have invested and bought a home in the exclusive area of Bel-Air, among their neighbors are Elon Musk and Nicolas Cage who have purchased property in Bel-Air years ago. Ben Affleck and Jennifer Lopez was the new addition to ther already star studded neighborhood of Los Angeles.

The Interior And Exterior Of The Bel-Air Mansion

The estate has ten bedrooms and seventeen bathrooms in total with multiple kitchens. The mansion has the classic design with wooden floors that has mixed marble design. Inside the luxurious mansion, a main show kitchen can be found that has a full catering kitchen and comes with two additional kitchens for the staff and the guests. The master bedroom suite is extraordinarily spacious that has its own sitting area with double baths and a massive walk in closet. There are only a total of seven bedrooms in the main building of the property which is just perfect with their combined five children. Among the seventeen bathrooms, there is a vintage pastel pink with gold look that has the vibe of an old-fashioned gold bath.

The mansion is known as the The Bellagio Estate and this gem of a beautiful house showcases art and luxurious design. Outside and around the main house are some exquisite grounds that contain a spacious main lawn overlooking the infinity-edge pool and a stunning pocket gardens scattered throughout the property. The estate is perfect for the reunited couple as it offers privacy for their family since it is surrounded by numerous trees and also gives them a beautiful view overlooking the golf course and the city.

Additional Amenities

The Bel-Air Mansion boasts additional amenities that the residents and guests of the mansion can enjoy. Among these additional amenities include a theatre for entertainment, gym for fitness, his and hers bathroom, a stunning pool and the wonderful city view that looks fantastic at night. Aside from the theatre room for entertainment, there is also a separate entertainment room where the guests can spend hours without getting bored. The outdoor infinity pool offers staggering views of the valley. There is also a guest house with an outdoor kitchen perfect for barbecue parties. The spacious estate has a gated entrance with a full motor driveway as well as a separate entryway for the service team.
