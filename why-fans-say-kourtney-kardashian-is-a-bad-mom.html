Fans Drag Kourtney Kardashian's Parenting, Sing Kylie's Praises

After over fourteen years on our screens, Keeping Up With The Kardashians came to an end, only to be replaced by a new Hulu series titled 'The Kardashians'. Now, fans are hooked on 'keeping up' with the Kardashian family, tuning in to watch the latest drama-filled episode on a regular basis.

RELATED: 10 Surprising Facts About Kylie Jenner & Travis Scott’s Relationship

Throughout the course of both series, fans have been given an inside glimpse into the chaotic and luxurious life of the family, including rocky relationships and family feuds, among other personal matters. The show has also included other intimate moments, such as the birth of Kourtney, Khloe and Kylie's children. Their sister, Kim, is also mother to four children whom she conceived with global rap star Kanye West, while Rob Kardashian has one child. However, as fans have watched the family grow up on their screens over the course of more than a decade, many have developed some strong opinions on some of their parenting skills.

Both Kylie And Kourtney Have Built Families Of Their Own

During the earlier seasons of Keeping Up With The Kardashians, fans got an extremely intimate view into Kourtney and Scott Disick's relationship. The pair dated for a total of nine years, and during that time, they decided to start a family of their own.

Although separated, they have three children together who were conceived during their relationship: Mason, Penelope and Reign. The birth of Mason was documented on Season 4 of Keeping Up With The Kardashians, giving fans an inside look at the much cherished moment.

Kylie, who is eighteen years younger than her sister Kourtney, has also started a family of her own with Travis Scott. Despite being young when she started a family, it seems as though Kylie has always had her heart set on wanting to be a mother from a young age.

These sentiments are echoed in a video that the fashion mogul posted on YouTube, documenting her first child's birth. In the video, her friends explain that Kylie has said that she 'always wanted to be a mom', and that she's wanted them 'since she was fifteen'.

In other interviews, she has also expressed that she felt twenty-five was the age that she hoped she would have children.

Her wish came true in February 2018. When the young socialite was just twenty years old, she gave birth to her first baby girl, Stormi, breaking the internet into a wild frenzy. Four years later in February 2022, Kylie gave birth to her second baby, who was initially called Wolf Webster.

However, the couple later announced that they had changed his name, meaning that his name is no longer Wolf. The new name has not been officially revealed.

Kylie has also opened up about her struggles post-pregnancy on an episode of The Kardashians, stating that she 'couldn't stop crying' for three weeks after her latest pregnancy.

She also expressed similar sentiments on an Instagram story, stating: “Postpartum has not been easy," and that the experience for her personally has been a little harder than with her daughter.”

Some Fans Don't Agree With Kourtney's Parenting Style

Of course, eagle-eyed fans are best known for their abilities to pick up on small snippets of information and clues. However, over several seasons, fans have made some judgmental comments in regard to Kourtney's parenting tactics.

One fan on Twitter commented, "Kourtney and Scott let them kids run all over them, it’s actually sad," while another stated: "Them kids can’t even behave while touring that house. How many times did they have to tell them to stop. I’m on the nanny’s side. I don’t know what Kourtney is doing while with her kids but parenting ain’t it. #KUWTK."

Fans have also taken to Reddit to discuss the topic, with one fan commenting: "I don’t agree with her parenting STYLE but I do think her kids are her whole world and she loves them with her whole heart," with another stating that they think Kourtney is quite 'hands off'.

The fiery debate sparked many opinions among the fan base, with other even discussing the parenting tactics of other family members, with each bringing up their own examples. However, others on social media platforms have also praised Kourtney and Scott's ability to co-parent their children together, highlighting the good.

Fans Have Praised The Way Kylie Raises Her Children

It seems that discussing parenting tactics is a hot topic among the Kardashian fan base, and it looks as though fans have been busy discussing the entire family.

More notably, since Kylie gave birth to Stormi, fans have been seemingly quick to notice that Kylie seems to of raised a very sweet and polite young baby.

Both online and on the show, Kylie and Stormi have been seen together on several occasions, including an array of sweet pictures that have been posted on social media.

One of these moments includes a sweet video, where Kylie leaves a bowl of sweet treats on the table and tells Stormi to wait while she goes somewhere. The catch? Stormi has to wait until she comes back.

Of course, Stormi waited patiently, and fans were in awe over how cute Kylie's baby girl was, praising her seemingly good manners and politeness. Others also noted in the comments that Stormi seemed to have good discipline, and praised Kylie for doing a good job.

This is not the first time that fans have praised Kylie's parenting skills, and it's not the first time onlookers have criticized Kourtney, either.
