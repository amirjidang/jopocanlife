Bunnie XO Admits That Her Relationship With Jelly Roll Wasn't Always Perfect And They Broke Up Befor

Highlights

  • Jelly Roll and Bunnie XO's genuine openness and honesty endear them to fans, despite their imperfect relationship.
  • The couple's journey from a breakup to renewing their vows in Las Vegas in 2023 is a testament to their love and commitment.
  • Jelly Roll publicly praises Bunnie XO as his best friend and anchor in life, highlighting their strong bond and mutual support.

Jelly Roll and Bunnie XO do not try to hide their relationship. Their openness and honesty about how they feel for each other and what they have been through endear them to fans. They have one of the strongest unions and have praised each other publicly many times. Still, in a new TikTok video, Bunnie XO revealed their relationship was imperfect. The couple even broke up in 2018.

"Who knew us breaking up in 2018, me moving back to Vegas & you coming to get me back – would have put us on this wild journey called life," Bunnie XO shared on the video.

What Did Fans Say About Jelly Roll And Bunnie XO's Breakup?

The text on the video also reveals how far they have come from their first meeting to the present day. "We finally committed to each other and did everything we promised each other the first night in 2016," the text reads. "Our castle in the sand had to crumble, so we could rebuild on solid ground. I yuh you so muchhh."

The couple met at the Need a Favor musician's concert in Las Vegas, but Bunnie XO (real name Alyssa DeFord) was in a relationship at the time. After she broke up with her former partner, she and Jelly Roll (real name Jason Bradley DeFord) started dating. They did not wait long before getting engaged (Jelly Roll proposed onstage during one of his concerts) and married at a Las Vegas chapel in 2016. However, the announcement that they split in 2018 surprised many, and fans have reacted with messages of support.

"Every relationship has to go through the storm at some point to find the light," one fan commented. Another shared similar thoughts, writing, "Girl, I get it. I actually divorced my husband and came back, and we have never been happier. It's me and him against the world. I couldn't be happier. I'm glad you found the same."

It appears many fans have found themselves in similar situations and understand how the couple could have a stronger relationship after a breakup. "Me and my husband are 8th Grade sweethearts, and we went through a 2-year separation and still fought our demons to be together till to this day," one TikTok comment reads. "What makes the relationship is the fight your both."

"I remember watching you through that breakup and then you two reuniting," a fan commented. "I can't believe it was that long ago already. Your love story is an inspiration!"

What Has Jelly Roll Said About His Relationship With Bunnie XO?

In 2023, the couple renewed their vows in Las Vegas. This was partly to show their commitment to each other again, but also because they disagreed on their original wedding date. Jelly Roll shared their reason for this with People in a December 2023 interview. "There was always a discrepancy with our anniversary because she thought it was on one day. I thought it was on another day. Neither one of us knew," he said.

They decided to renew their vows on September 1; as the musician explained, it "felt right because we thought it was between the 30th and 31st."
