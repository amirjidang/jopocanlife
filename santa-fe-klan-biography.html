Santa Fe Klan Biography

Santa Fe Klan age is 23 years old. Santa Fe Klan is a Mexican rapper.

The rapper started his career on January 8, 2017 with the release of his debut record, Por Constumbre. Since his debut, he has released over 121 singles, three EPs, and seven mixtapes and albums.

Aside from his own music he also runs the music group/label 473 Music, which features other prominent up-and-coming Mexican rappers like Tornillo. In fact, Santa is seen as one of the leaders in a new crop of Mexican rappers that are making their known these days.

The rapper’s success can be seen in his many social media platforms and his massive following. Santa has over 9.6 million followers on Instagram, 10.4 million subscribers on YouTube, and over 8.4 million monthly listeners on Spotify.

What Is Santa Fe Klan Real Name?

Santa Fe Klan real name is Angel Jair Quezada Jasso. Angel is a common Latinx name.

The name’s meaning is the same as its meaning in English, which is an Angel, i.e. a servant of God. The middle name, Jair, comes from a Hebrew word that means “he shines” or “he’s enlightened.”

The rapper’s first last name Quezada is also a common Mexican name. Quezada is a form of the name Quesada, which means fertile land, and the name comes from the town of Quesada in Jaen, Andalucia, in Spain.

As for his last name, Jasso comes from the Jasso family from Zisa, Lower Navarre. The rapper’s stage name, Santa Fe Klan, is a reference to the neighborhood he grew up in, which bears the same name.

Where is Santa Fe Klan From?

Santa Fe Klan family is from Guanajuato, Mexico. More specifically, he is from the Santa Fe neighborhood in Guanajuato.

Located in Central Mexico, the city exists within a state of the same name. The city bears a lot of historical and cultural significance due to its many historic sites like castles and museums, and for being the place where the Mexican War of Independence first war was fought.

Despite the city’s historical importance, the rapper has stated that the neighborhood he came from is a poor neighborhood and he is among the first from there that has achieved any sort of fame and success. As such, he bears his stage name proudly for himself, his family, his neighborhood, and now his fans.
