Tori Kelly Biography and Wikipedia

Tori Kelly born as Victoria Loren Kelly is an American singer, songwriter, actress, and record producer. She gained fame after posting videos on YouTube as a teenager and debuted as a semi-finalist on the ninth season of American Idol in 2010.

She independently released her self-produced debut EP in 2012, Handmade Songs by Tori Kelly. Her second EP and first major-label release, Foreword in 2013, followed soon after. Kelly’s debut studio album Unbreakable Smile in 2015 peaked at number two on the Billboard 200 chart in the United States with its lead single, “Nobody Love”, marking her first appearance on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.
