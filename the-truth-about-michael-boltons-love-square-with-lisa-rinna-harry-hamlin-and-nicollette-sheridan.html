The Truth About Michael Bolton's Love Square With Lisa Rinna, Harry Hamlin and Nicollette Sheridan

The love square between Lisa Rinna, Harry Hamlin, Nicolette Sheridan and Michael Bolton has had fans mesmerized for years. The story behind the way this unfolded has made its way to the Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills and has even been talked about by Andy Cohen.

It seems everyone is talking about what transpired between this foursome, and the details are pretty juicy. Too Fab reports that the dynamics between these four people have had an impact on the way each of their lives has developed, and fans want to know every detail.

10 Harry Hamlin And Nicolette Sheridan Were Married

Harry Hamlin and Nicolette Sheridan were married, but not for long. The two met on the set of the 1990 made for television movie called Deceptions, where they co-starred alongside one another. In September of 1991, the two were married and seemed to be on their way to a beautiful life together. Unfortunately, Harry Hamlin ended up filing for divorce in August of 1992.

9 Nicolette Goes To A Concert While Hamlin Is Away

Harry Hamlin and Lisa Rinna seem to agree on the way his marriage to Nicollette Sheridan ended, in spite of the fact that Sheridan denies the allegations. It has been revealed that while Harry Hamlin was away in Canada, Nicolette Sheridan went out for a night of fun at the Michael Bolton concert. She ended up leaving with... Michael Bolton, and the two were said to have begun their affair that night.

8 Michael Bolton And Nicolette Sheridan Have A Fling

By all accounts, it appears there were sparks flying between Nicolette Sheridan and Michael Bolton that lasted for more than just the one night. Information has surfaced revealing that the night at the concert was the first of many the two shared together. While married, Nicolette Sheridan continued to date Michael Bolton.

7 Nicolette Sheridan Claims Their Marriage Was Already Over

Nicolette Sheridan's story changes right here. She claims that she and Hamlin had already ended their marriage, and that he had opted to stay behind in Canada, knowing they were "done." She says their marriage was already over, and stated that the ended it "nose to nose." She even went so far as to remind Hamlin, and her fans, that Bolton was a friend of hers who had even attended her wedding. Playing down the affair to seem like nothing more than two pals hanging out didn't fly with fans.

6 Harry Hamlin Slams Back

When Nicollette Sheridan tried to make it sound as though her marriage to Harry Hamlin was something the two had agreed upon, Hamlin struck back in a big way. He issued a Tweet that revealed his clear anger over the situation, and he pointed the finger at Sheridan for stepping out on their marriage at a very sensitive time in his life, after the passing of his mother. He refuted all claims that he and Sheridan had mutually discussed their marriage dissolving and seemed angry and hurt by her actions.

5 Harry Hamlin And Lisa Rinna Meet

Harry Hamlin and Lisa Rinna met in the most unsuspecting way. Lisa was working in an eyeglass store, and after closing the store at 10 pm, she went to drop off the keys to the owner, who happened to be dining with Harry Hamlin. Hamlin tells People; "It was sparks right away," and the two started dating shortly thereafter.

4 Lisa Rinna Thanks Michael Bolton For Freeing Up Harry Hamlin

Lisa Rinna has been openly thankful of Michael Bolton's interactions with Nicolette Sheridan. She credits him for being the reason Harry Hamlin became single, and hers for the taking. Rinna has approached Bolton in person and thanked him for having a relationship with Sheridan that ultimately led to her divorce from Hamlin. This was a critical step in the development of Rinna's life.

3 Michael Bolton Thinks Rinna Is Sincere

Michael Bolton has admitted that Lisa Rinna has approached him with gratitude. This was back when Rinna and Hamlin just had one daughter. He tells the media; "I was aware of Lisa because I would see her at the Beverly Hills Hotel, at the Polo Lounge. And she would make a beeline over to me with this big smile on her face. And give me this big hug. I really didn't know her personally. And, and she explained why…because she said, ‘If it weren't for you, I would have never gotten together with Harry and had a child.'"

2 Bolton And Sheridan Try Again... And Fail

Michael Bolton and Nicolette Sheridan began dating in 1992, and they actually stayed together until 1997. They had a good five-year stretch, but ultimately ended up going in different directions. In 2005, they sparked their romance back up again and even got engaged in 2006. Their engagement and romance lasted two years, before they walked away from one another for good.

1 Rinna And Hamlin Remain Happily Married

Lisa Rinna and Harry Hamlin remain happily married today, so all seemed to end very well for them. They share two daughters, Amelia Gray and Delilah Belle. The two seem to be enjoying their lives to the fullest. As for Nicolette Sheridan, she was married to Aaron Phypers for six months before filing for divorce. Michael Bolton... well he's running the Celebrity Dating Game now and helping other celebrities find love.
