Why Did Ty Christopher Head Kill Two Women? Jacksonville Murder Case Takes Tragic Turn

Ty Christopher Head, a 22-year-old young man, killed two women in the San Marco area of Jacksonville on August 9, 2023. He murdered these women with several gunshots and killed himself near Nashville Tan a day after the double murder.

The double murder mystery took a tragic turn on September 13, 2023, when the Jacksonville Sheriff’s Office announced the clearance regarding the murder had been resolved through an official statement.

Alan Parker, chief of investigations for the Jacksonville Sheriff’s Office, stated that The incident happened on August 9, when 28-year-old Paige Pringle and 58-year-old Tara Baker were found deceased near railroad crossing Jacksonville.

The incident happened around 1:30 AM when Piage was on her way home while driving at Hendrix Avenue. She stopped her car at the railroad crossing to pass the train, and Tara Baker was behind her on her bike.

Ty Head was driving behind these women in his grey Volkswagen Passat; Ty suddenly came out of his car, shot Paige and Tara several times with his gun without any reason, and disappeared from the crime scene in his vehicle.

Mr Parker also stated that both victims died immediately on the spot, and the investigators did their best and began an investigation to find the murderer of this tragic double murder.

Homicide detectives and the FBI worked together to find him and the suspects they got from the crime scene, including his vehicle’s model and colour. The detectives soon identified Ty Head as a primary suspect through his car, which matched the same automobile used on the crime scene.

Unfortunately, when they reached to catch him, the force found that Ty Head also killed himself in Nashville, Tennessee, the next day after the double murder. Ty Head was born on October 10, 2023, and died at 22.

The officer claimed Ty Head committed suicide by shooting himself with his nine-millimetre Handgun. According to Forensic reports, the suspects and evidence found from the double murder scene were matched with Ty Head’s body.

It officially stated and clarified that Ty had murdered Paige and Tara. However, the reason behind these murders is still unknown as the sheriff said Ty blocked all the ways to know the circumstances regarding the murder by committing suicide. The case has not been closed yet, and the investigation is still underway.
