Amy Wegmann Biography

Amy Wegmann is a renowned American Journalist and Actress who was born and brought-up in the United States. Currently, she works at Fox 4 News in Fort Myers, Florida as an Evening News Anchor since July 2014.

Prior to joining the Fox 4 News team, she worked at ABC Network, Lifetime, USA Network as a News Anchor and Principal Actor on ‘Secrets and Lies’ Show. She also worked as a Freelance Journalist and as a Reporter and Journalist at Fox 4 News from September 2005 to March 2012.
