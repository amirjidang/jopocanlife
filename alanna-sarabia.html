Unveiling the Love Story, Alanna Sarabias Husband Jonathan Reyes

Alanna Sarabia, an American journalist, producer, and television personality, was born on December 29, 1981, in North Texas, United States.

She is the daughter of Lorraine Sarabia and Rick Sarabia and has three brothers: Adrian, Vincente, and Marcus. Alanna is particularly close to her parents, who have celebrated more than 40 years of marriage.

Alanna started her television career in San Antonio after graduating from South Grand Prairie High School and earning a Bachelor of Arts in Electronic Media from Texas State University. In November 2021, she began serving as TEGNA’s afternoon and evening traffic news presenter.

Before that, from May 2016 to March 2021, she co-hosted and produced “Good Morning Texas” while working with TEGNA. Initially joining WFAA in May 2016, she moved to Dallas. Until 2021, Alanna co-hosted “Good Morning Texas” with Jane McGarry.

Previously, Alanna was the local NBC affiliate’s San Antonio Living host and worked as an entertainment reporter representing CW 35. She became Miss San Antonio and a Silver Dancer for the San Antonio Spurs during this time. Despite giving up some enjoyable hobbies, such as being the “CW Insider” and co-host of San Antonio Living, she remained dedicated to her work.

Who is the Husband of Alanna Sarabia?

Alanna Sarabia’s personal life includes a decade-long marriage to police officer Jonathan Reyes. They tied the knot on October 30, 2021, at St. Paul the Apostle Church in Horseshoe Bay, Burnet, Texas, with Castle Falkenstein hosting their reception.

Their engagement had taken place in February 2021. The couple welcomed a beautiful daughter in 2022. While Alanna is open about her professional life, she maintains privacy regarding her husband’s identity. In her free time, Alanna enjoys travelling to create lasting memories.

Her adventures have taken her to Cuba, Mexico, Spain, and Italy. She aspires to visit every National Park in the United States. Beyond travel, she enjoys dancing, skiing, and playing the piano. Alanna practices her faith by attending the Catholic church and is known for her piety.

As a renowned news personality, Alanna Sarabia has earned significant respect and fame throughout her career. Her estimated net worth is approximately $1 million.
