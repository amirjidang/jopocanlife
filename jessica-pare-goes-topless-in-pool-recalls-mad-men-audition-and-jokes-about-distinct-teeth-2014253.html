Jessica Pare Goes Topless In Pool; Mad Men Star Jokes About Her Teeth

Why hello, Mrs. Draper! Jessica Pare has dropped her chic ’60s dresses for simple lace lingerie — and at times, nothing at all. In the April issue of Esquire magazine, the Mad Men actress flaunts her svelte figure as she bares her cleavage in a swimsuit, and goes topless in a pool. Far removed from her days back in Montreal, Pare also dishes on her AMC audition, and how her life would have been much different if she didn’t become Jon Hamm’s (second) onscreen wife.

“I really struggled,” the Canadian-born actress, 33, said in the issue of trying to make it in Hollywood. “I was ready to move back to my parents’ place, ready to get a job at my friend’s bar back home and just pack it in.”

Before landing the role of secretary-turned-actress Megan Draper, Pare had wrapped Season 1 of the WB series Jack & Bobby in 2004. Like other actors, she was on to the next audition, but for her, landing a job meant a lot more than just another paycheck. It turns out, she needed an employer willing to sponsor her in order to receive a visa.
