Lisa Marie Presley News - Us Weekly

Name:Lisa Marie Presley

Hometown:Memphis, TN

Birthday:February 1, 1968

Died:January 12, 2023

About

Daughter of music legend Elvis Presley and Priscilla Presley, Lisa Marie Presley was born in Memphis, Tennessee, in February 1968.

Lisa Marie was a daddy’s girl until her father’s death in 1977. After his death, her high profile romances with Michael Jackson and Nicolas Cage, along with her battle with drug addiction made her the subject of much media attention. In her own career as a singer, Lisa Marie released multiple albums and singles. Her debut album, To Whom it May Concern , was released in 2003 and debuted on the Billboard Hot 100 at No. 5. The “Lights Out” singer also released several singles that include digitally enhanced duets with her father.

The Storm & Grace musician shared twin daughters Finley and Harper, born in 2008, with ex-husband Michael Lockwood, whom she was married to from 2006 to 2021. She also shared two children from her previous marriage to Danny Keough: daughter Riley, born in 1989, and son Benjamin, born in 1992, who died by suicide in 2020.

In January 2023, the singer-songwriter was rushed to the hospital after suffering cardiac arrest at her Calabasas, California, residence. Her mother announced Lisa Marie’s death hours later.
