Mickie Rourkes Drug Dealer In The Wrestler Arrested Last Night

In what could definitely be considered ironic, Scott Siegel (the actor who played Randy The Ram’s drug dealer in "The Wrestler") was arrested in New York last night following a crazy police chase.  Siegel was charged with distribution of steroids, which is what he provided Mickie Rourke’s character in the movie, and assaulting an officer.

Siegel was apprehended in Eastchester, New York following a car chase through Westchester.  During the chase, he rammed several police cars and once stopped, he abandoned his SUV and tried to flee on foot. 

His arraignment should be held today for distribution of steroids and assaulting an officer.
