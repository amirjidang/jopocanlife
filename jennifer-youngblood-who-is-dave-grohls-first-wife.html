Jennifer Youngblood: Who is Dave Grohls first wife?

Dave Grohl, the frontman of the Foo Fighters and a consummate lovely guy, has been married to Jordyn Blum since 2003. Grohl’s first wife, photographer Jennifer Youngblood, and their marriage are less well-known.

Grohl has only talked publicly about his ex-wife a few times, and each time he does, he keeps it brief, admitting his own mistakes but never criticizing her.

Youngblood, unlike Grohl, is a private person rather than a public celebrity, so the truth is that we know very little about her.

According to The Guardian, adultery played a role in Dave Grohl’s divorce from Jennifer Youngblood in the 1990s.

Despite the fact that their sexual connection did not work out, Grohl has no grudges against his first wife. Rather, it appears that the timing wasn’t quite right.

“I was 25, my wife was even younger. We weren’t ready to get married,” he once told Melody Maker (via NME).

“I miss her a ton, she’s the funniest goddamn weirdo you ever met in your life. Absolutely adorable. It would have been better had we not got married. But you live and you learn.”
