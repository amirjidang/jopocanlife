The House Next Door

Tristan’s teenage bedroom in his childhood home is haunted by a faceless woman. He learns that his wife’s great grandmother, Naomi Dancy, was murdered in the house next door. Intrigued by the connection, Tristan delves into the family’s history to uncover the truth behind the haunting.

Naomi Dancy’s murder in the house next door adds an eerie dimension to Tristan’s ghost story podcast. The supernatural encounters in his childhood bedroom and the tragic events surrounding Naomi’s death intertwine, leaving Tristan with a gripping mystery to explore.
