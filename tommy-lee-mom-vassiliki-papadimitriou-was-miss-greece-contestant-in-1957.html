Vassiliki Papadimitriou Was A Miss Greece Contestant In 1957

Tommy Lee mother Vassiliki Voula Papadimitriou was a contestant on Miss Greece in 1957. She gave birth to Lee on October 3, 1962.

Born on August 8, 1939, Vassiliki is best known for her brief stint in Greece’s national beauty pageant and for giving birth to her rock and roll star of a son.

In the pageant, the singer’s mother lost out to Dora Kostidou, who won the title of Miss Greek during that year. The former contestant would marry David Lee Thomas Bass, the father of her two children, Tommy and Athena.

Though she gave birth to Tommy on Greek soil, the family would end up moving to the United States two years after Lee’s birth and the former contestant would have Athena in the US.

Despite her marriage with David eventually ending, she spent the rest of her life in California, and finally passed away on March 9, 2012, in Calabasas.

Tommy Lee Father

Tommy Lee father David Lee Thomas Bass was a US Army Sergeant. He was born on April 30, 1924.

A US native, Bass was born in the state of Minnesota to John Samuel Bass and Julie E. Sanders. He was born, raised, and studied in Itasca, Minnesota.

David Lee Thomas Bass

Joining the US army at a young age, Bass fought in World War II and the Korean war. However, his son remembers him best for being a great father.

He met and married the mother of his children while stationed in Greece. As stated above, they eventually moved back to the US.

Bass would go on to leave his first wife and marry another woman, Lois Lavonne Aultman. Sadly, he passed away in 2001.

Tommy Lee Sister

Tommy Lee sister Athena Lee is also a musician and plays the drums like her brother. She was born on December 8, 1964.

Unlike her brother, she was born in the United States. She started her musical career a bit later than her brother, at age 20. 

Athena Lee

By 22, Athena was playing in various bands like the all-female outfit Hardly Dangerous and the punk band, Butt Trumpet. Aside from music, she also worked in music-related television shows like VH-1’s Behind the Music, where she was a guest commentator, and E! True Story Hollywood Story.

She has been married twice: first to Scott Atkins, a former bandmate, and then to James Kottak, once a drummer of the band Scorpion. The drummer has three children: one from John Guerrero in 1991, then with her first husband in 1992, and the third with Kottak in 1997.

Tommy Lee Family

Tommy Lee family consists of his two sons Brandon Thomas Lee and Dylan Jagger Lee. Brandon was born on June 5, 1996, and Dylan on December 29, 1997.

Both of his sons were born to his third wife, Pamela Anderson. Their initial years together were fraught with problems due to the parents’ custody battle, which resulted in joint custody in 2002.

Brandon Thomas Lee

Brandon Thomas Lee is an actor, model, and producer. Most recently, he produced a documentary about his mother.

Lee’s oldest son is closer to his mother than the rockstar and has been her rock for many years now.  Unfortunately, his relationship with his father has been tenuous at best, and the two were in a public feud for much of 2018.

Dylan Jagger Lee

Dylan Jagger Lee is also a model like his mother and brother. He has worked with brands like Dolce & Gabbana, Hugo Boss, and many more. 

He has followed in his father’s footsteps in his passion for music, but his tastes are more towards the electronic side, though he knows how to play the guitar and piano. The younger son is currently a member of the music duo, Motel 7.
