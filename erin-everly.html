Erin Invicta Everly (born November 8, 1965 in Los Angeles, California) is an American former model. She is the daughter of Don Everly of the musical duo The Everly Brothers, and the ex-wife of Guns N' Roses front man Axl Rose. She was the inspiration for the Guns N' Roses hit "Sweet Child o' Mine".
