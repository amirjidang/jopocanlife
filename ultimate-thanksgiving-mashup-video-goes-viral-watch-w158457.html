Ultimate Thanksgiving Mash-Up Video Goes Viral: Watch

https://www.youtube.com/embed/YzDxt6axexs

Watch me sip… my chardonnay nae! The Holderness family created the ultimate mash-up of some of 2015’s hottest songs to celebrate Thanksgiving. The amazing clip has already gone viral with over 1 million views since it was originally posted on Thursday, Nov. 19.

The YouTube-loving clan, whose Christmas Jammies video also went viral in 2013, combined Adele’s “Hello,” Drake’s “Hotline Bling,” and Silento’s “Watch Me (Whip/Nae)” into a hilariously fun tune about the annoyances of Turkey Day.

The video starts off with the family fighting over the dinner table as mom Kim Dean Holderness sings, “Now watch me sip… my chardonnay nae ‘cause I’ve been cooking since 4:30.”

The clip then cuts to the dad, who sings, “Jell-O, it’s supposed to be dessert” to the tune of Adele’s megahit, while his kids opt for green Jell-O instead of green beans as their side dish.
