Cody Lee Nickson, a name etched in the annals of reality television, has carved out a unique journey from military service to the winner’s circle on “The Amazing Race.” As fans continue to follow his endeavors, let’s dive into the multifaceted world of Cody Nickson, exploring his time on Big Brother, his net worth, and the intriguing facets of his personal life.

Early Life and Background

Born on April 13, 1985, in Lake Mills, Iowa, Cody Nickson later found residence in Plano, Texas. A man of diverse experiences, Cody’s journey began with his enlistment in the United States Air Force at the tender age of 17. His military service extended to the United States Marine Corps, where he served as a 0311 Rifleman, completing combat tours in Iraq and Afghanistan. Cody earned an honorable discharge in 2012, a testament to his dedication and service.

Television Stardom

Big Brother 19

Cody Nickson stepped into the limelight during the 19th season of Big Brother. From the very beginning, his strategic prowess and alliances captured attention. Despite facing eviction on Day 23, Cody’s resilience brought him back into the game through the Battle Back Showdown. His journey continued with notable wins and strategic moves, ultimately earning him the title of America’s Favorite HouseGuest and a $25,000 prize.

Big Brother 20

While Cody didn’t participate as a contestant, he and his fiancée, Jessica Graf, graced Big Brother 20 as hosts for the second POV competition, reaffirming their connection to the Big Brother family.

The Amazing Race

Cody and Jessica’s reality TV journey reached its pinnacle on The Amazing Race 30, where they emerged victorious, claiming both the $1,000,000 grand prize and an additional $5,000 prize. Their triumph marked a historic moment as the first former Big Brother contestants to conquer The Amazing Race.

Personal Life

Cody Nickson’s personal life weaves a tale of love, family, and anticipation. Engaged to Jessica Graf on February 13, 2018, the couple tied the knot on October 14, 2018. Their family expanded with the arrival of a daughter born on March 17, 2019. In May 2020, Cody and Jessica joyfully announced their second pregnancy, welcoming another daughter on October 5, 2020.

Controversy and Beyond

Not immune to scrutiny, Cody Nickson faced criticism for transphobic remarks during Big Brother 19. Despite social media backlash, he chose not to issue an apology. Additionally, during the COVID-19 pandemic, Cody sparked controversy by using the term “Scamdemic” in promoting his coffee brand, a phrase associated with pandemic conspiracy theories.

Net Worth and Stature
