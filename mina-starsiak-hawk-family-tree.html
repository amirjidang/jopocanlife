Mina Starsiak Hawk Family Tree

Mina Starsiak Hawk family tree includes father Casey Starsiak, mother Karen Laine, 5 siblings, and husband Steve Hawk. Mina has 2 kids Jack and Charlotte Hawk.

The first season of Good Bones went on air on March 2016 and its latest season eight will premiere in August 2023.

With the success of the show, Starsiak established her own home furnishing named Two Chicks and a Hammer. The mother-daughter duo created a buzz in the real estate business with their home renovation projects.

Mina and Karen Good Bones family members have successful careers in their respective fields.
