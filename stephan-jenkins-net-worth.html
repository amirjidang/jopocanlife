Early Life and Education

Stephan Jenkins, born on September 27, 1964, in Indio, California, had an interesting upbringing that influenced his musical journey. Attending Henry M. Gunn Senior High School, Jenkins continued to nurture his love for music, honing his skills and developing as an artist. It was during his teenage years that he truly became enamored with the power of music and the art of songwriting.

After high school, Jenkins pursued higher education at the esteemed University of California, Berkeley. He decided to study English literature, which further expanded his creative horizons and enriched his understanding of storytelling and lyrical expression. Jenkins graduated from the university with his degree in 1987, armed with a profound knowledge of literature and an unwavering passion for music.
