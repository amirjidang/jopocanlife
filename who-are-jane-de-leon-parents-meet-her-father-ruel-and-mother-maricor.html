Who Are Jane De Leon Parents? Meet Her Father Ruel And Mother Maricor

Jane De Leon is an actor, singer, and model born to her father, Ruel Florencio De Leon, and her mother, Maricor Benitez De Leon.

The actress is buzzed with excitement to portray the famous superhero of Philipines, Darna. The talented actress, Lana, playing the privileged role of Darna, has made people come forward, showering her with praises and admiration.

The parents of Lana are quite proud to witness the success and grandeur that their daughter has received. And she is equally lucky to have support and love from parents.

Who Are Jane De Leon’s Parents? Meet Her Father, Ruel, And Mother, Maricor.

The talented actress Jane De Leon was raised by her loving father, Ruel Florencio de Leon, and her supportive mother, Maricor Hearty Benitez-De Leon.

Lana is the youngest in her family, with five siblings, four being her half-siblings on her father’s side. Her half-siblings include Karen, Carol, Kirox, and Ned. Jane is super close with her family and likes to spend time with her siblings. Her brother Francis is closet to her, and she considers him her go-to person. The brother-sister duo shares an unbreakable bond.

Ruel loved all his children and supported their every decision until the end. But her father lost his battle with lung cancer in 2016, leaving his family behind. Jane was deeply affected by her father’s passing, and on the occasion of father’s day in 202, she opened up about her father and her feelings towards him on an Instagram post.

She revealed that she misses her father a lot and how it is difficult not to have him around. She also seemed disheartened that she couldn’t share her success and enjoy activities with her father. She reminisced their fishing and bonding moments and how she wished to have him with her and her family. Lastly, she prayed for her father to always guide and watch over her.

Lana is also very close with her mother. Maricor has done a remarkable job of raising her children. Lana mentions that her mother has guided her life, teaching her to be independent and strong and acknowledging her self-worth. Maricor is a selfless woman who pushes all her children toward better light and encourages them to be better people.

Both parents were strict in her childhood, as is often seen in Filipino upbringing. The actress slipped that she often used to sneak to play outside behind her parents due to strict rules in her household. Despite the stringent environment in her family, both her parent used to shower her kids with affection in their ways. Her father, when alive, had set the tradition of cooking himself on Christmas eve. The family would gather around the table to taste his unique dishes.

What Is The Ethnicity Of Lana De Leon? Is She Filipino?

Lana De Leon belongs to the Filipino ethnicity. She was born in Quezon City, Philipines.

She gained her primary and secondary education in Philipines. Sampaguita Elementary School and San Roque Elementary School served as Jane’s institutes of primary education. She attended Sampaguita Village National High School for her secondary studies.

8 Facts To Know On Lana De Leon

  • Her full name is Jane Florence Benitez De Leon
  • She was born on November 22, 1998, and is now 23.
  • She is named after her grandmother Jane and aunt, Flor. Her parents would have chosen Hyacinth, Amapola, Jade, or Joey if her name hadn’t been Jane Florence.
  • She competed in Teen Supermodel Philippines in 2013 and reached the semifinals.
  • She made her debut in an uncredited role in the romantic comedy-drama Just The Way You Are, in which Liza Soberano and Enrique Gil costarred
  • On ABS-noontime CBN’s program It was Showtime in 2016, Jane made her official debut as a member of the girl group GirlTrends, presently known as GT.
  • The popular prime-time television series Halik, which ran from August 2018 to April 2019, gave Jane her most significant break in the industry.
  • Jane is co-managed by Tyrone Escalante Artist Management and Star Magic, the ABS-CBN talent management division (TEAM).
