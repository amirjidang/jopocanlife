Lil Nas X And 9 Other Male Celebs Who Are Pushing The Boundaries Of Fashion

It's certainly no secret that celebrities tend to be fashion trendsetters, but when we think of the rich and famous and the fashion industry more often than not we think of female celebrities. However, in the past couple of years, there have been more and more male stars who decided to rock rather fun looks that are not only gender-bending — but also incredibly creative.

Related: Kim Kardashian's 10 Most Iconic Fashion Moments

Frankly, we love seeing actors, musicians, and reality television stars who are not afraid of speaking up about toxic masculinity, and rocking clothing that they love — and not what society tells them they should wear. From musicians like Lil Nas X and Harry Styles to Hollywood stars like Billy Porter and Jared Leto — keep on scrolling to see who made the list!

10 Lil Nas X

Kicking the list off is 21-year-old rapper Lil Nas X who rose to fame in 2019 thanks to his hit "Old Town Road". Lil Nas is definitely a rapper who likes to have fun with fashion and fans get to witness that during every red carpet appearance he makes. Lil Nas absolutely loves bold colors and he tends to wear monochromatic intricate outfits that are certainly not minimalistic!

9 Harry Styles

Let's move on to former One Direction member Harry Styles whose style actually became more noticeable after the musician parted ways with the band. The 27-year-old started experimenting more with fashion once he became a solo artist, and today Harry is known for being a major trendsetter — whether it comes to wearing pearl necklaces or rocking nail polish!

8 Billy Porter

Next on the list is Hollywood star Billy Porter whose career started on Broadway in the 90s.

Related: Kendall Jenner's 10 Best Steet Fashion Looks

Over the past few years, the 51-year-old has been very vocal about toxic masculinity — and when it comes to fashion Billy is definitely one of the most famous male celebs who loves rocking stunning dresses and gowns. Frankly, the actor always looks absolutely incredible, no matter what he wears!

7 Jacob Elordi

While some male celebs tend to go all out — others experiment with fashion in small doses and one of those stars is Euphoria actor Jacob Elordi. The 23-year-old has recently been spotted wearing items like cross-body bags and earrings — accessories that are traditionally considered more feminine. Considering that the show Euphoria had such a major impact on the beauty and fashion industry, we wouldn't be surprised if it also helped the young star explore his style more!

6 Jared Leto

Next on the list is 49-year-old Hollywood star Jared Leto who has certainly been known for rocking plenty of funky outfits over the years. The actor and musician was never afraid of making a statement with his clothing choices and over the years he has always remained true to his own style — which often includes very bold colors and funky patterns!

5 Jonathan Van Ness

A true male fashion icon is certainly reality television star and hairdresser Jonathan Van Ness who rose to fame in 2018 thanks to the Netflix show Queer Eye. Those who follow the 34-year-old on Instagram have surely seen plenty of his incredible outfits — and one thing that Jonathan absolutely loves is rocking sky-high heels! Above are two of his iconic everyday outfits and there is certainly no doubt that the reality television star knows how to put together a look!

4 Pharrell Williams

Let's move on to 48-year-old musician Pharrell Williams who has over the years fought against toxic masculinity and embraced his feminine side — including fashion which is traditionally deemed as girly.

Whether it's rocking a wrap dress for a photoshoot or running an errand in a vibrant pink coat, Pharrell rocks whatever he thinks looks good and it definitely makes him happy!

3 Jaden Smith

Another young artist on today's list is musician and actor Jaden Smith who grew up in the eye of the public. The 22-year-old may have looked up to his father Will Smith, but when it comes to fashion there is no doubt that Jaden is the more stylish one. Over the years fans got to see the star experiment with plenty of fun clothing items such as skirts, dresses, purses, and as seen above — Jaden is not afraid of pastel colors!

2 Ezra Miller

Next on the list is 28-year-old actor Ezra Miller best known for starring in movies like The Perks of Being a Wallflower and Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them. The actor has always had a very unique fashion sense, and over the years Ezra rocked plenty of funky clothing items that one might think are rather feminine. Above, the star can be seen rocking a floral look with makeup — as well as a very puffy dress!

1 Young Thug

We kicked the list off with rapper Lil Nas X and we're wrapping it up with yet another rapper — this time we're talking about Young Thug. It's no secret that in the rap industry men tend to stick to certain kinds of fashion — which is why it's very refreshing to see talented rappers experimenting with their style. 29-year-old Young Thug is definitely not afraid of dresses and florals and above he can be seen rocking both!
