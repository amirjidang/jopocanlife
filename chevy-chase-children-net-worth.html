Everything We Know About Chevy Chase's Children And Their Net Worths

Highlights

  • Despite Chevy Chase's controversies, his three children are accomplished in their own right.
  • Cydney is a singer-songwriter, Caley is an actress, and Emily works in wildlife conservation.
  • While they may not have reached their father's level of fame, they all seem to be financially stable.

After Saturday Night Live turned Chevy Chase into a superstar, he became a beloved comedy actor. Since then, Chevy Chase's controversies have dominated his reputation. After all, Chase's infamous SNL fight with Billy Murray and the offensive things he reportedly did on the set of Community have made him one of Hollywood's most hated stars.

Despite all of Chase's drama, he has three kids who just see him as their father. With that in mind, many observers are curious about who Chase's children are and what is known about their finances.

This article will reveal who Chevy Chase's three children are. Then, the article will look at how much money Chase's children seem to have.

Who Are Chevy Chase's Children?

After previously getting married and divorced twice, Chevy Chase walked down the aisle with Jayni Luke in 1982. Still together to this day, Chevy Chase and his wife, who changed her name to Jayni Chase, had three children together.

According to public reports, all three of Chevy Chase's daughters have followed in his footsteps in one way or another. After all, two of Chevy Chase's daughters are artists and the third seems to have an affinity for being in the public eye.

Chevy Chase's eldest daughter, Cydney Chase, was born in 1983. Clearly someone with a passion for art, Cydney Chase has grown up to be a singer-songwriter. Undeniably blessed with a beautiful voice, Cydney Chase regularly uploads videos of herself singing on social media.

Cydney Chase's Instagram profile also lists her as a "grateful daughter", "Nature Lover", and someone who loves "Painting and Yoga".

Chevy Chase's second daughter Caley Chase was born in January 1985. While all of Chevy Chase's daughters followed his example to a certain degree, Caley Chase followed her father into the acting world.

According to Closer Weekly, Caley Chase made her onscreen acting debut in 2005. Since then, Caley Chase has appeared in a selection of small projects. More importantly, Caley Chase seems to have a real passion for helping to bring plays to fruition.

On Caley Chase's personal website, there is a very revealing resume section. According to her resume, Caley Chase has been directing regularly for the theater since 2016. Caley Chase also lists credits from a variety of other departments, including assistant directing, producing, and more.

Chevy Chase's youngest daughter, Emily Chase, is the one who has a career that is nothing like her father's. Born in September 1988, Emily Chase told the New York Post in 2022 that she works in "wildlife conservation".

However, Emily Chase seems to share one major attribute with her father, they are both very outspoken. According to Closer Weekly, Emily Chase speaks out on social media consistently and she touches on her views about controversial topics like criminal justice reform.

In March 2024, Chevy Chase announced some huge news for his family. Chevy Chase was very excited to reveal his daughter Emily Chase was carrying his first grandchild.

What Is Known About Chevy Chase's Net Worth And How Rich His Daughters Are?

After spending decades as a movie and television star, Chevy Chase accumulated an impressive fortune for himself. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Chevy Chase has a $50 million fortune as of April 2024.

Even though Chevy Chase's daughters are all accomplished, none of them have reached the level of fame their father did. As a result, it shouldn't surprise anyone that there is no way to know for sure how rich Chevy Chase's daughters are.

As of April 2024, there aren't any reports about how rich Cydney Chase is. The only thing that has been reported is that Cydney Chase makes a decent amount of money as a singer. Similarly, very little is known about how rich Caley Chase is.

Unlike her elder sisters, something concrete can be reported about Emily Chase's financial situation. Thanks to a 2022 real estate sale, it seems clear that Emily Chase has access to a decent amount of money at least.

In 2012, Chevy Chase spoke to The Huffington Post UK about his career. During that interview, Chase admitted he regretted passing on movies like Forrest Gump, Ghostbusters, and Animal House for financial reasons. "So all those I regret only because they made huge amounts of money and I would be very wealthy."

According to The New York Post, Emily Chase and Chevy Chase sold a Brooklyn condo they owned together for $989,000 in 2022. The father and daughter purchased the condo together in 2018 for $912,000.

Chevy Chase Reportedly Has A Son Who Likely Doesn't Exist

Since Chevy Chase has been a famous actor for decades, there is no doubt that he has led a fascinating life. However, one of the most interesting things about Chevy Chase appears to be that the media is convinced he has a son who never actually existed.

When anyone Googles how many children Chevy Chase has in April 2024, the results come back that he has four children. Google and other websites all report that Chevy Chase's eldest child is his son named Bryan Perkins.

According to Closer Weekly, Bryan Perkins is the son that Chevy Chase had with an unidentified woman.

However, according to what Emily Chase posted on her Instagram Stories in March 2024, Chevy Chase doesn't have a son at all.

After it was announced that Chevy Chase was going to become a grandfather, Emily Chase posted an image from People's article on the news. In the image, Emily Chase underlined a mention of Bryan Perkins and stated she doesn't have a half-brother by that name.

"Beyond hilarious that we have a mysterious, fake half brother who is routinely reported as being part of the family. Who IS he? Where did this amazing rumor start? Why isn't his last name Chase? ❤️ u imaginary Bryan."
