Selena Gomez Wears Control-Top Pantyhose Just Like Us

Can't keep her tights to herself! Selena Gomez showed off an area of her tights that most of Us would rather cover — the control top.

While arriving in New York at JFK airport on Wednesday, January 20, Gomez, 23, looked cozy and casual in a chunky knit turtleneck sweaterdress that she paired with leather over-the-knee boots by Jimmy Choo, a wide-brimmed felt hat and a blush crossbody bag.

Making sure her legs were shielded from the dropping temperatures in NYC, the "Hands to Myself" singer put on a pair of sheer black tights and managed to make the control-top portion — the part that smooths away any lumps and bumps — look stylish.

Gomez wore her hair in a straight bouncy blowout, and kept her makeup minimal and natural for her airport exit, opting for a fresh light pink lip.

The Revival singer flew to NYC ahead of her appearance as the musical guest on the January 23 episode of Saturday Night Live, with host Ronda Rousey. "Sooo I'm performing on @nbcsnl on 1.23!! What is life?? I couldn't be more excited," she wrote on Twitter on January 5. "GUYS.. SNL!!"
