Inside The Fast And Furious Stars Garage

Christopher Brian Bridges, aka Ludacris, is a rapper and actor mostly known for appearing as Tej Parker in the blockbuster Fast and Furious series. He is a successful rapper, having won three Grammy awards, and some of his tracks are also featured in the Fast And Furious movies. Ludacris’s massive success in the industry has earned him an incredible net worth of $30 Million. Like all gearheads, he spends most of his income maintaining a multi-million-dollar car garage.

Ludacris owns a desirable set of super luxury cars from the pioneer brands like Ferrari, BMW, Mercedes, Rolls-Royce, Acura, etc. Below is a list showing the eight finest vehicles in the rapper cum actor’s garage.

Acura Legend – $30,000

This might not be the most expensive or luxurious car on the list, but this car has a lot of significance in Ludacris’s life. Like most celebrities, Ludacris also has a humble beginning. During the mid-1990s, before he shot to fame, Ludacris bought his first-ever car in the form of a silver Acura Legend. He is very attached to his first car and drives it around to date, clocking in more than 250,000 miles on the odometer as per Hot Cars. The Acura Legend is powered by a 3.2-liter V6 block engine, generating up to 200 Horsepower.

Acura NSX - $160,000

Ludacris’ second Acura purchase significantly upgraded his beloved Acura Legend. The Acura NSX is often regarded as one of the most underrated cars in this segment, boasting a 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 hybrid engine that generates 573 Horsepower. This speed machine can quickly accelerate from 0-100 in a mere 2.9 seconds, making it a perfect ride for someone like Ludacris, who owns one in white.

Mercedes-AMG G63 - $156,000

Ludacris combined his gearhead and romantic sense and bought an all-white Mercedes-AMG G63 for his wife, Eudoxie. He surprised her by inviting her to the sets of Fast And Furious 8 and presenting the luxury SUV to her. The AMG, also known as G-Wagon, is powered by a 563 Horsepower twin-turbo 5.5L V8 engine that can zoom from 0-100 in 5.3 seconds, as reported by CarHP.

Mercedes-Maybach GLS 600 - $200,000

Yet another Mercedes in Ludacris’s garage, the Mercedes-Maybach GLS 600, is an absolute beast. Ludacris had this Mercedes custom built for him, flaunting dual-tone exteriors and the seats covered with Nappa-leather. He was so drawn to this powerful SUV that he bought it before it was put into dealerships. Additional features in the customized GLS include a pair of 12.3-inch screens, a Burmester 3D surround sound system, along with heated and ventilated seats with a massage function providing the pinnacle of comfort.

McLaren MP4-12C - $230,000

Ludacris gifted himself this supercar in November 2013, flaunting the car on his Instagram handle. The $230,000 coupe is painted in black, while the wheels are driven by a powerful 592 Horsepower 3.8L M838T V8 engine accompanied by a 7-speed automatic transmission. The car is relatively light owing to its carbon fiber body, which accompanies the engine to take the super-fast vehicle from 0-100 in just 3.1 seconds as per Fastest Laps.

Lamborghini Huracan Spyder - $250,000

A luxury car collection is incomplete without a Lamborghini, so Ludacris had to get one. He owns a Lamborghini Huracan Spyder, one of the world's most famous supercars. The Huracan is made for speed and is powered by a 602 Horsepower 5.2-liter V10 engine, as reported by 21 Motoring. The supercar has a lightweight aluminum body and can catapult to a speed of 100 in under 3.4 seconds.

Rolls-Royce Dawn - $356,000

Rolls-Royce is the flagbearer of super luxury and comfort, and one of them graces Ludacris’s garage in the form of a dazzling Rolls-Royce Dawn. Ludacris bought this ultra-luxurious beast around 2019, cashing out a massive $356,000, and like most of his cars, the Dawn is also painted in white. The Dawn can seat four passengers and has state-of-the-art features like self-adjusting suspension, active cruise control, and adaptive headlights. Even though it is built for luxury and comfort, it is pretty fast, boosting from 0-100 in just 4.9 seconds.

Rolls-Royce Phantom Drophead Coupe - $450,000

Boasting a cost of almost half-a-million dollars, the Rolls-Royce Phantom Drophead Coupe is the most expensive and luxurious car in Ludacris’ incredible fleet of supercars. The grand tourer offers the absolute pinnacle of luxury and has a striking white interior with brown trim made from a combination of teak, hardwood, and stainless steel. The exterior has a 2-toned color with a design inspired by the 100EX. The Phantom Drophead possesses a 6.75-Liter V12 controlled by a 6-speed transmission, and it is not just the most expensive car in Ludacris’ garage but also one of the most costly Rolls-Royces ever.

Ludacris’ garage consists of some of the most exquisite cars available. He seems to have a strong affinity for white color, and many of his cars have a white finish. Despite being a multi-millionaire with various vehicles, he still maintains his first car, the Acura Legend. The standout car in Ludacris’s garage seems to be the Rolls-Royce Phantom Drophead Coupe, which boasts ultra-luxury, a powerful engine, and a price tag only a few can afford.
