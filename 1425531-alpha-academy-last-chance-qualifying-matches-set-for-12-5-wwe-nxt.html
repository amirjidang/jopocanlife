Alpha Academy, Last Chance Qualifying Matches Set For 12/5 WWE NXT

Three matches have been announced for next week’s WWE NXT.

Just days before NXT Deadline, two four-way matches will take place to determine who will receive their last chance to qualify for the Iron Survivor Challenge matches. On the men’s side, it’ll be Tyler Bate vs. Joe Coffey vs. Carmelo Hayes vs. Eddy Thorpe. For the women, it’ll be Fallon Henley vs. Roxanne Perez vs. Thea Hail vs. Kiana James.

Additionally, Alpha Academy’s Chad Gable, Otis, and Maxxine Dupri have officially challenged Meta-Four’s Noam Dar, Oro Mensah, and Lash Legend to a six-person mixed tag team bout.

Below is the updated lineup for December 5:

  • : Tyler Bate vs. Joe Coffey vs. Carmelo Hayes vs. Eddy Thorpe
  • : Fallon Henley vs. Roxanne Perez vs. Thea Hail vs. Kiana James
  • : Alpha Academy vs. Meta-Four

WrestleZone will have complete coverage of the show as it airs next week.
