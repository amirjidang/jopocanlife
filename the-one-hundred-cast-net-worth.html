The sci-fi, post-apocalyptic drama that is based on the novel of the same name has become one of television’s most popular and trending shows. The 100 follows the story of 100 criminal adolescents that are sent to earth to after spending their entire lives on The Ark, a space habitat created after a nuclear holocaust has ravaged the Earth.

The young adults are sent to see if Earth is inhabitable to live in after 97 years, but they soon discover that not only has the Earth changed; there were some that survived, and what was once a simple mission turns into an on-going struggle to survive.

The show has some well-known actors in the industry, while most of the main cast are new faces. How much do these survivors of the human race make? Here are the net worths of the cast of The 100.

Eliza Taylor - $2 Million

Eliza Taylor portrays the main character, Clarke Griffin, on The 100. Clarke is the leader of the original 100 young adults, and she constantly tries to do what's right for her people, even ate extenuating lengths. Taylor has received praise for her acting on the show, but before this, the Australian-actress was featured in other prominent shows.

Taylor got her big breakthrough on the Australian soap opera, Neighbours, playing the role of Janae Timmins. She starred in the soap from 2005-2008, and then went on to nab roles in the films: The November, Thumper, and Christmas Inheritance. Taylor shocked her fans on June 7, 2019, when she announced on Twitter that she and her co-star, Bob Morley, secretly wed. Taylor's net worth is estimated to be $2 million.

Bob Morley - Net Worth: $3 Million

The members of "the 100" not only depended on Clarke's smart leadership skills, but also Bellamy Blake's tough demeanor and unrelenting will to fight for what he believed in. Bellamy is played by Australian-actor, Bob Morley. Morley, began his career in 2006 when he cast in the Australian television show, Home and Away, where he portrayed Drew Curtis. Following this, he joined the cast of The Strip and also Neighbours.

In 2019, Morley made his directorial debut when he directed the episode "Ashes to Ashes"  in the 6th season of the show. Morley, who is married to his on-screen co-star, Eliza Taylor, has a net worth of $3 million.

Paige Turco - Net Worth: $5 Million

Paige Turco portrays the confident Dr. Abigail, "Abby" Griffin, Clarke's mother, and the medical supervisor on The 100. Before this, many may remember Turco from her roles in various other shows and films. Turco starred in two popular daytime soap operas, Guiding Light and All My Children, from the years 1987-1991, as well as the teen-drama Party of Five. The actress also had roles in the films Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles II: The Secret of Ooze, Dead Funny, The Favor, and Urbania.

In addition to guest roles in the shows, Law and Order: Special Victims Unit, Rescue Me, and Blue Bloods; Turco's net worth rounds out to be $5 million.

Isaiah Washington - Net Worth: $500,000

Before he took on the role of chancellor of the ark, Thelonious Jaha on The 100, Washington was best known as Dr. Preston Burke on the long-running prime-time soap, Grey's Anatomy. Washington portrayed Burke from 2005-2007 before being fired from the show after a confrontation on set.

Washington then reprised his role in a guest appearance on Grey's Anatomy in 2014. He has also starred in Law & Order, Dead Presidents, Love Jones, Ghost Ship, and Hollywood Homicide, rounding out his net worth to be $500,000. Washington left The 100 in 2018.

Henry Ian Cusick - Net Worth: $1 Million

Henry Ian Cusick isn't a stranger to fighting in unknown lands on television. Cusick played Desmond Hume from 2005-2010 on the American television-drama Lost. Prior to this, Cusick appeared in the films and tv shows, Scandal, Hitman, The Girl on the Train, and The Mentalist.

On The 100, Cusick portrays Marcus Kane, a former councilor on the ark who assist the teens surviving on earth. The actor has left the show after six seasons and reportedly has a net worth of $1 million.

Marie Avgeropoulos - Net Worth: $500,000

This Canadian-actress and model has become one of the deadliest women on television, but her character's story was a complicated one. Marie Avgeropoulos portrays Octavia Blake, the younger sister of Bellamy, who was hiding under the floors of the ark because families were only allowed to have one child. Octavia was sent to the ground with her brother, but she found her true identity in the grounders that live on Earth rather than her friends from space.

The actress's breakthrough role was in 2010 when she was cast as Kim Rhodes in Hunt to Kill. Following this, Avgeropoulos made several guest appearances in various shows and films and has an estimated net worth of $500,000.

Richard Harmon - Networth: $100,000-$1 Million

John Murphy has come a long way. From being a self-loathing troublemaker to a true companion, Richard Harmon has embodied the essence of Murphy's psyche and his on-going struggles to do what's right for everybody else. Harmon has also been featured in the films, Trick r' Treat, Percy Jackson and the Olympians: The Lightning Thief, The Age of Adeline, and the television shows CSI: Crime Scene Investigations, Bates Motel, and Fringe.

Harmon's sister, Jessica, also stars on The 100 as the character Nylah. Harmon's net worth ranges from $100,000 to $1 million.

Lindsey Morgan - Net Worth: $ 1 Million

What would The 100 be without the talents of mechanic extraordinaire Raven Reyes? Fans probably wouldn't want to find out. Lindsey Morgan has excelled as the beautiful and strong-willed Raven whose smarts and innovative ideas kept the 100 and others alive. The 29-year-old actress had a few small roles before making her debut as Kristina Davis on General Hospital in 2012. Morgan was nominated a Daytime Emmy for her performance.

In addition to her appearances in How I Met Your Mother, Franklin & Bash, and Beyond Skyline, Morgan's net worth is estimated to be $1 million.

Adina Porter - Net Worth: $2 Million

Adina Porter has graced the television screen for quite a while. The actress first rose to prominence during her time on the two HBO series, those being the fantasy-horror show, True Blood, as Lettie Mae Thorton, and The Newsroom, as Kendra James. Porter also joined a coven of witches, a haunted house, a spooky forest, and a terrorizing cult in four different seasons of the FX anthology series, American Horror Story. The actress is also a theater-vet, having appeared in several off-Broadway plays.

Porter now portrays the warrior, Indra, who has taken Octavia under her wing while also leading her people to survival. Porter has an estimated net worth of $2 million.

Zach McGowan - Net Worth: $4 Million

Before becoming the slain King Roan on The 100, Zach McGowan became known for his unforgettable roles and voice-over work. McGowan's big break came in 2011 when he joined the cast of Shameless as the innocent, naive Jody. Following this, McGowan was cast in the film Dracula, Untold and then landed the main role in the Starz original series Black Sails.

In 2016, McGowan portrayed Roan, King of the Ice Nation, who the 100 find an ally in. McGowan's net worth is estimated to be $4 million, which is a culmination of his acting and voice roles.
