Sally Pressman Welcomes Baby Boy!

It's a boy for Sally Pressman! The Army Wives actress and her husband, actor David Rogers, welcomed their first child on Wednesday, April 10, her rep confirms exclusively to Us Weekly.

"Sally would just like everyone to know that she gave birth to a healthy baby boy and they are blissfully happy enjoying their perfect baby," the rep says.

News of the 31-year-old Lifetime star's pregnancy broke last October, a little over a year after the couple tied the knot. According to a wedding announcement in the New York Times, the nuptials went down at Bacara Resort and Spa in Santa Barbara, Calif., in September 2011.
