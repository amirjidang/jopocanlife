Kendra Wilkinson Says She Was a Lesbian for Two Weeks in High School

News flash! Kendra Wilkinson revealed to her former assistant and good friend Eddie that she was once a lesbian. In a sneak peek of this Friday’s Kendra on Top episode, Wilkinson informs her pal that she dated a girl when she was younger.

“Did you know I was gay at one point in my life?” Wilkinson, who is now married to former NFL pro Hank Baskett. “I dated a girl. In high school, I dated a girl [for] like two weeks.”
