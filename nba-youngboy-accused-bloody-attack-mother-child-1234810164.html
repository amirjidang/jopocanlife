NBA YoungBoy Accused Of Ordering Bloody Attack On Mother Of His Child

NBA YoungBoy is being accused of ordering two women to physically assault the mother of his child, which allegedly occurred while the victim was holding their child.

The alleged victim, Arcola, who shares a son with the rapper, took to social media on Monday (Nov. 6) to blast the rapper for ordering the attack and putting their son in harm’s way. According to Arcola, the assault occurred at NBA YoungBoy’s home in Utah, where he’s currently on house arrest.

“Watch who you have kids by,” she began, sharing a photo of herself with blood on her face from a cut above her eye, presumably sustained during the alleged attack. “My son dad let his whoreS yes whores with an S cause it was 2 of them weak a** ho*s. He gave them an order to get me lol you know bi**hes who never came from sh*t gone do it. My son was in my hands. His dad just sat back and watched smh.”

She then made a string of posts detailing the events that led up to the alleged altercation, which she says stemmed from a disagreement over travel arrangements for their child. “Mind you I dropped my son off the end of August he wanted me to come get him 2 weeks into him being there. So I told him keep him 2 more weeks. He was pissed lmao I just feel like if you ain’t gone help me financially you gone help physically.

“He’s so unreasonable so happy this is all over with now,” she added. “I asked his wife [Jazlyn Mychelle] if she can ask him to get just kaell flight and he said no. Keep in mind the flights was 1300 for both of ours round trip and all i asked was for him to pay for kaells. ni**a still couldnt do that. So i was just like f**k it. im tired! anytime he ask for his son i drop him off and pay for the tickets. bi**h you dont help with sh*`t why i gotta keep paying for the flights?”

Arcola said that after requesting to retrieve their son from YoungBoy’s home, he told her to wait two additional weeks before coming to pick him up, only to later tell her that she could visit the child on weekends.

“LMFAO them drugs got his mind gone,” she wrote, referencing the rapper’s alleged substance abuse. The mother then says she took it upon herself to come to YoungBoy’s home to pick up their son, only to be ignored, prompting her to search the home for the child herself. “I finally found him and his whores was trying to take my son out of my hands smh I cannot make this up.”

The alleged victim says that, despite the women’s best efforts to take her son away from her during the altercation, she refused to let go and ultimately left the premises with the child, who was apparently uninjured during the skirmish. Arcola also says that she doesn’t plan to press charges against the rapper or the women involved in the alleged skirmish, which she says resulted in her getting five stitches to close a laceration above her eye.

“I would be wrong if I pressed charges on yall for doing me like this with my baby in my hand. But karma gone eat yall a**es up,” she wrote. “I got my son back I’m happy that’s all that matters fr. 5 stitches later. And her punk a** fist ain’t do that shorty had sum. They kept trying to take kaell from me. I cried so hard I even screamed so loud like you’d thing I was mental. I was holding my baby sooooo tight I was never letting my son go to swing on you low life whores. I came for my son and left with him.”

NBA YoungBoy has been involved in several incidents involving the mothers of his various children. In 2022, Yaya Mayweather, the daughter of boxing legend Floyd Mayweather Jr. and the mother of one of the rapper’s children, plead guilty to stabbing the mother of another of NBA YoungBoy’s children during a dispute in 2020.
