Birthname Paul Thomas Anderson Birthdate June 26, 1970 Birthplace Los Angeles, California, U.S. Height 6' (1.83 m) Profession Director Net worth $50,000,000 Source of Wealth Film Directing, Writing and Producing Nationality American Country United States

Paul Thomas Anderson is an American film director, screenwriter and producer. His Net Worth is estimated at $50 million. Paul Thomas Anderson is famous for his work including Magnolia, There Will Be Blood, Boogie Nights and The Master. His last movie release was in 2014, American Hustle.

Paul Thomas Anderson was born on June 1970 in California, Studio City. He grew up in San Fernando Valley, and attended several schools including Campbell Hall School and Montclair Prep. He started film making at a very tender age of 12 on his dad’s camera. His movie production career began at New York University. He focused mainly on music movies, television movies and game shows. His first full feature was released in 1996, titled Hard Eight, successfully screened at Cannes Film Festival.

Paul Thomas Anderson got a breakthrough in 1997 through the movie Boogie Nights. New Line guaranteed next film production after financial success of this film.
