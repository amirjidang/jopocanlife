Sebastian Maniscalco was born to his parents Salvatore Maniscalco and Rose Maniscalco. Sebastian was raised in Illinois.

Sebastian Maniscalco Parents Have Italian Origins

Sebastian Maniscalco was born to Italian parents Salvatore and Rose Maniscalco in Illinois.

He was born on the 8th of July, 1973, in the Chicago suburb of Arlington Heights.
