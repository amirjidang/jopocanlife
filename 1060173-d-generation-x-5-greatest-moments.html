D-Generation Xs 5 Greatest Moments

Leading into WrestleMania 14, ‘Stone Cold’ Steve Austin was the hottest star in the company and getting ready to rise to a whole new level. Adding Mike Tyson, The Baddest Man On The Planet and one of the biggest superstars in the sporting world certainly made the main event a big box-office attraction.

Tyson teased tension with DX, but ended up joining them in what was a big twist leading up to the main event. It put Austin against more odds. The alliance between Tyson and DX would last all but 27 days, when he turned on them at WrestleMania 14 and helped Austin win the WWF Championship. It was a great story for a great time.
