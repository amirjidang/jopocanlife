Who Is Behati Prinsloo?

First and foremost, who exactly is Behati Prinsloo? Despite the model's name dancing all over recent news headlines, some fans of the Maroon 5 band member and members of the public alike are still unsure of who exactly she is.

Behati Prinsloo was born in South Africa in 1988 and grew up attending school in the country. At the young age of 16, she was spotted by a modeling agency during a visit to Cape Town with her grandparents.

This revelation would eventually lead to Prinsloo dropping out of school and signing with the agency, marking the start of what would become a highly successful modeling career.

Since becoming a model, she has become best known for her work as a Victoria's Secret lingerie model and often appeared in their fashion shows between the years 2007 and 2015. Another highlight from her career was being the face of Victoria's Secret PINK line in 2008, as well as becoming an 'Angel' the proceeding year.

She has also appeared on the face of several highly-established fashion magazines, such as Vogue, GQ, and Elle. Her highly successful career has allowed her to establish a name for herself within the industry and has allowed her to mix with a large pool of A-List celebrities.
