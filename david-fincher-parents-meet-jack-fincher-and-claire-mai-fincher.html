David Fincher Parents: Meet Jack Fincher and Claire Mai Fincher

David Fincher’s parents are Jack Fincher and Claire Mai Fincher. Claire Mae Boettcher was a mental health nurse from South Dakota who worked in drug addiction programs.

David Andrew Leo Fincher was born on August 28, 1962, in Denver, Colorado, United States of America. When he was two years old, David Fincher and his family moved to San Anselmo, California, where filmmaker George Lucas was one of their neighbours,
