Corey Stoll Net Worth: Corey Stoll is an American actor who has a net worth of $3 million. Corey Stoll was born in New York City, New York in March 1976. He is best known for his role as Peter Russo in the TV series House of Cards. He graduated from Oberlin College and New York University’s Graduate Acting Program at the Tisch School of the Arts.

Stoll first appeared in the short Okenka in 2001. His television debut came in the series CSI: Crime Scene Investigation in 2001. He has starred in the movies North Country, Lucky Number Slevin, The Number 23, Brief Interviews with Hideous Men, Push, Helena from the Wedding, Salt, Midnight in Paris, The Bourne Legacy, The Time Being, Victoriana, C.O.G., Decoding Annie parker, Non-Stop, and Glass Chin. From 2010 to 2011 he starred as Detective Tomas “TJ” Jaruszalski on the series Law & Order: LA. In 2013 Stoll starred as U.S. Representative Peter Russo in the Netflix series House of Cards. In 2014 he starred as Ephraim Goodweather in the FX series The Strain. In 2014 Stoll was nominated for a Golden Globe for House of Cards. He has also been nominated for a Critics Choice Television Award, Independent Spirit Award, and a Satellite Award.
