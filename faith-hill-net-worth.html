Faith Hill has an estimated net worth of $80 million. Faith Hill is a successful musician, songwriter, singer, actress and performer. Hill's first singing stint started off in her adopted parents' Baptist Church where she discovered her love for music.

In 1986 she moved to Nashville to pursue a music career. After being discovered by Martha Sharp while singing backup for Garry Burr, Hill was quickly signed and shortly, she released her debut album, Take Me As I Am in 1993 which was welcomed with immediate success. The album's first single "The Wild One" remained number one on the country billboard charts for four weeks, yet this was only the beginning of Faith Hill's infinite amount of success.

Since her Take Me As I Am Album, she has released over 10 more albums resulting in 40 million albums sold worldwide as well as won eight grammys, awards from The Academy of Country Music, Country music awards, dozens of hit songs and much more. Recently she has also settled down with fellow country music star, Tim Mcgraw.
