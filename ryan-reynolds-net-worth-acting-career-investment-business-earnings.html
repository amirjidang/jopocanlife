Millions Of Ryan Reynolds' $350 Million Net Worth Doesn't Come From Acting

Highlights

  • Ryan Reynolds has achieved immense success in Hollywood, but the majority of his impressive $350 million net worth comes from his various business ventures.
  • Reynolds earned substantial paychecks from his roles in films like Deadpool and 6 Underground, contributing to his financial success in the entertainment industry.
  • In addition to his Hollywood career and business endeavors, Ryan Reynolds is actively involved in philanthropy.

Ryan Reynolds has enjoyed a remarkable Hollywood career, but the bulk of Ryan Reynolds impressive net worth comes from his other business endeavors. Ryan Reynolds' film careeer spans from comedic successes like The Proposal to blockbuster hits such as Deadpool and its sequel. He portrays the titular character Wade Wilson in Deadpool movies, which have grossed over $1.5 billion at the box office with a third on the way.

Beyond his achievements in the entertainment industry, Ryan Reynolds is also a savvy businessman, holding ownership stakes in diverse ventures, including technology companies, beverage enterprises, and even a soccer club. While Ryan Reynolds' salary for film projects is consistently in the substantial eight-figure zone, the bulk of his $350 million net worth comes elsewhere.

How Much Of Ryan Reynolds' Net Worth Comes From Acting?

Ryan Reynolds has been acting since the early '90s; however, in 2016, Reynolds found critical and commercial success with Deadpool. Reynolds secured his first significant movie payday with 2011's The Green Lantern, earning a reported $15 million salary for the movie. The movie turned out to be one of the most significant box-office disappointments in history, managing to gross only $220 million despite a hefty production budget of $200 million. However, Ryan Reynolds’s paycheck cleared and he met his future wife Blake Lively on set.

Ryan Reynolds significantly boosted his earnings from playing Deadpool, despite a relatively modest upfront payment. While rumors circulated that he initially earned around $2 million for the first film, Reynolds negotiated substantial backend points, resulting in a total payday of $22 million due to the movie's immense success. For Deadpool 2, Ryan Reynolds' earnings climbed even higher, with estimates suggesting he could have made up to $40 million. The details of his compensation for Deadpool 3 are unclear, but given the remarkable success of the first two films, it's anticipated to be Reynolds' most substantial payday in his acting career.

His compensation soared with the 2019 Netflix film 6 Underground, where he brought in an impressive $27 million. In 2021's Red Notice, where he starred alongside Dwayne Johnson and Gal Gadot, Ryan Reynolds earned $20 million, according to Forbes. Continuing his success, Reynolds earned another $20 million for his role opposite Will Ferrell in the 2022 film Spirited. These substantial paychecks contribute significantly to Ryan Reynolds' overall financial success with a $350 million net worth in the entertainment industry.

How Ryan Reynolds' Net Worth Became $350 Million

Ryan Reynolds' net worth is not solely from his Hollywood career, but also from his successful side hustles. In 2020, his gin brand, Aviation American Gin, proved to be a lucrative venture.

Reynolds sold his stake in the brand to Diageo for an impressive $610 million. Vanity Fair reported that Diageo would pay Ryan Reynolds $335 million upfront, with an additional potential payday of $275 million contingent on Aviation's performance over the next 10 years. As part of this deal, Reynolds also secured an "ongoing ownership stake," ensuring his continued involvement and potential future profits in the success of the gin brand.

Mint Mobile, recognized for offering some of the most affordable prepaid plans in the U.S., was recently acquired by T-Mobile for a substantial sum of $1.35 billion. Reynolds, holding an estimated 25% stake in Mint Mobile, is set to gain approximately $300 million from this acquisition, as reported by the Wall Street Journal. This strategic move adds another significant financial success to Reynolds' portfolio of business ventures.

In 2020, Ryan Reynolds, along with fellow actor Rob McElhenney, made headlines by purchasing Wrexham Association Football Club for $2.5 million. Subsequently, in June 2023, it was revealed that Reynolds was part of an investor group that acquired a 24% stake in the Alpine Formula 1 race team, contributing to a total investment of $218 million in the team.

What Ryan Reynolds Does Besides Acting

Beyond his entertainment career and several business endeavors, Ryan Reynolds actively engages in philanthropy to support various causes. His contributions extend to a Canadian water charity aiding Indigenous young adults, assistance for displaced families from Ukraine, donations to a Vancouver food bank, and an annual holiday campaign known as the "aesthetically challenged" sweater initiative. This campaign, featuring Seth Rogen, raises funds for the SickKids Foundation.

Reynolds has been involved with The Michael J. Fox Foundation since 2009 and, in 2022, established The Creative Ladder, a nonprofit aimed at enhancing accessibility to creative careers for individuals from underrepresented communities.

Alongside his wife Blake Lively, Reynolds has generously donated half a million dollars to Water First Education & Training Inc., a Canadian charity training Indigenous youth in water treatment plant operations to provide safe water to their communities.
