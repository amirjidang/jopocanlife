WWE Issues Statement On Kevin Owens Injury

Kevin Owens didn’t appear on RAW last night after being thrown off the top of the steel cage by Braun Strowman at Extreme Rules. WWE issued a storyline injury update on Owens’ condition saying that Owens suffered “multiple injuries, including bruising of the shoulder and back, bruised ribs and a neck strain.” WWE also said that Owens would be evaluated later on in the week.

Owens is still scheduled to appear at WWE Live Events.
