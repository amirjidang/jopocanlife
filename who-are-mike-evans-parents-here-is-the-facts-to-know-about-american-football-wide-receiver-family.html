Who Are Mike Evans Parents? Here Is The Facts To Know About American Football Wide Receiver Family

Mike Evans was born to his parents Heather Kilgore and Mike Evans Jr., in Galveston, Texas. Keep reading the article to discover more details on his parents and family.

Mike Evans is an American football wide receiver with the National Football League’s Tampa Bay Buccaneers (NFL).

Evans is the first wide receiver in NFL history to have at least 1,000 receiving yards in each of his first seven seasons.

Who Are Mike Evans Parents?

Mike Evan was born to his parents, Heather Kilgore and Mike Evans Jr., on August 21, 1993. 

Similarly, Evans grew up with his parents in Galveston, Texas, United States. 

Evans had a tough childhood. Heather Kilgore, his mother, was 14 years old when he was born.

Furthermore, his father, Mickey Evans, allegedly had a history of abusing his mother, but this was a family secret for a long time.

Meet Mike Evans Mother Heather Kilgore And Father Mike Evans Jr.

Heather Kilgore and Mike Evans Jr. are the mother and father of Mike Evans, as previously stated. 

Even though Heather had kept her husband’s abuse a secret, her brother, Sam Kilgore, was aware of it. 

When Mike was nine years old, his uncle, Sam, murdered his father because he was violently abusive to Heather.

Later, Sam got sentenced to 38 years in jail for the murder. Evans eventually forgave his uncle and paid him multiple visits to prison. 

Additionally, Heather also worked for the University of Texas Medical Branch at Galveston University for some years.

Mike Evans Family Explored

Mike Evans had a horrible upbringing in his family. There is no denying that Evans’ childhood experiences influenced him.

Evans has founded the Mike Evans Family Foundation, which grants scholarships to underprivileged children and aid to victims of domestic abuse.

Likewise, Mike has a sister who is anonymous now. He does not have a biological brother, but he considers Terran Petteway, a professional basketballer, to be his brother. 

Evans and Petteway grew up in Galveston, Texas, and Petteway presently plays professional basketball for Dinamo Sassari in the Italian league.

Meet Mike Evans Wife On Instagram

According to his Instagram pictures, Mike Evans is happily married to his wife, Ashli Dotson Evans. 

Reportedly, the lovely couple got engaged on 19 December 2014 and married on 13 February 2016. 

The married duo has been married for more than five years now. Mike frequently posts photographs with his wife on his Instagram. 

Similarly, Mike and Ashli share three wonderful children named Ariah Lynn, Amari Thomas, and Mackenzie.

In December 2017, they established the Mike Evans Family Foundation to aid victims of domestic violence.
