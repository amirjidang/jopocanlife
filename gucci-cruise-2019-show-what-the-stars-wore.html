Gucci Cruise 2019 Show: What the Stars Wore

Gucci gang! Saoirse Ronan, Salma Hayek, ASAP Rocky and more stars hit up Gucci’s Cruise 2019 show on May 30, 2018, in Arles, France. The surprise twist: The show took place at Alyscamps, a Roman cemetery. Gucci commented on its location via their Instagram account stating, “Alyscamps is a Roman cemetery, but it’s also not a cemetery, it was a promenade, it became a walk in the 1700s; it is hybridized, it does not look like a cemetery because it is and it isn’t. I like things that seem like something but are not.” The post continued to describe the aesthetic for this year’s show: “Flames and fire divide the runway which features candles burning on iron candelabras for the #GucciCruise19 fashion show on the Promenade Des Alyscamps in Arles. The ancient site, where Roman citizens were buried, is the setting for the new collection.”

Stars in attendance dressed to the nines in embroidered suits, lace details, daring bras and lots of florals — bringing all the drama the setting called for. Scroll down to see their looks!
