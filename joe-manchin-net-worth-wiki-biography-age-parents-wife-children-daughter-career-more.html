Joe Manchin Career

Joe Manchin served as a member of the West Virginia House of Delegates from the 31st district from December 1982 to December 1986. He became a member of the West Virginia Senate for ten years, from December 1986 to December 1996.

From January 2001 till January 2005, he acted as the 27th Secretary of State of West Virginia. He became the 34th Governor of West Virginia and served his tenure from January 2005 to November 2010.

Manchin headed the Chair of the National Governors Association for five months in 2010. He was the ranking member of the Senate Energy Committee from January 2019 to February 2021.

He acting as the Senator from West Virginia since November 2010 and is also heading the Chair of the Senate Energy Committee since February 2021.
