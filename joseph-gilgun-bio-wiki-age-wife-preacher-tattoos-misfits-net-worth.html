Joseph Gilgun Biography

Joseph Gilgun is an English actor born in Chorley, Lancashire. He is famously known for his role as Eli Dingle in Emmerdale, Jamie Armstrong in Coronation Street, Woody in This Is England (2006), and Rudy Wade in Misfits.

He also starred in Preacher as the Irish vampire Cassidy from 2016 to 2019. From 1994 to 1997, Joseph starred Jamie Armstrong in Coronation Street. He also presented a feature on Wish You Were Here at the age of 14 in 1998. For a while in his teenage years, Joseph took a break from acting and only acted part-time in Hanky Park and Borstal Boy.
