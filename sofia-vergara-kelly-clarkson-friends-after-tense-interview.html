What Happened During Kelly Clarkson And Sofia Vergara's Interview?

Kelly Clarkson's Sofia Vergara interview was awkward to watch because Vergara was clearly offended by the talk show host's comments. Instead of acknowledging that Vergara was upset and changing the subject, Clarkson kept going.

According to Page Six, Clarkson commented on Vergara's appearance in Griselda and said, “I feel like they only changed your nose or something." When Vergara said that was "crazy," Clarkson said, “It looks slight." When the actress explained that she spent hours in hair and makeup, Clarkson kept suggesting that it was a small change.
