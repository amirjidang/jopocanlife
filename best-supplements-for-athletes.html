Best Supplements for Athletes: Our Top 6 Picks

What Are Sports Supplements?

Legal sports supplements have the potential to support your body and mind before, during, and after workouts. Supplements are not designed to replace a healthy diet but rather an accompaniment to one.

They come in many forms, such as powders, capsules, pills, and gels, and have been formulated to help:

  • Resolve nutritional deficiencies
  • Meet nutritional needs, e.g., protein
  • Meet fitness goals
  • Enhance exercise performance

There are numerous types of supplements for sports nutrition, including:

  • Amino acids
  • Vitamins
  • Minerals
  • Herbs
  • Botanicals
  • Food and plant concentrates

The kind of supplement you need will depend on your goals and your chosen sport.

Do elite athletes take supplements?

While elite athletes can perform well and build muscle without supplements. However, as athletes push their bodies to the limits, supplements may help provide a boost to energy levels, muscle power, aerobic capacity, and recovery.

Data suggests that elite athletes use dietary supplements more than their non-elite counterparts. This could be because for an individual to perform at their best. They require a certain amount of calories, fluids, carbohydrates, protein, fat, vitamins, and minerals—supplements help them maintain these nutritional needs.

However, it’s important to remember that no supplements are a substitute for a solid dietary foundation.

How do I choose the right supplements for me?

The use of supplements (fitness, weight loss supplements, or others) remains controversial, and when it comes to what you’re putting into your body, it’s always worth erring on the dies of caution.

Before starting any new supplements, speak to your healthcare provider or sports dietician about their suitability for you and ask the following questions:

  • Do the claims made by the supplement make sense?
  • Is there scientific evidence available for the ingredients?
  • Is the supplement safe, legal, and free of banned substances?
