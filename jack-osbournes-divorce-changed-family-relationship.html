How Jack Osbourne's Divorce Changed The Famous Family Forever

For anyone who wasn't around during the early 2000s, it is hard to explain just how popular MTV's The Osbournes was at the time. A reality show based around Ozzy Osbourne and most of his family, The Osbournes, became an extremely influential show.Even though some people still question how real The Osbournes actually was, it was so popular that shows like Keeping Up with the Kardashians followed in its footsteps. On top of influencing other shows, The Osbournes seemed to have a huge impact on its stars.Related: The Chaotic Truth About Filming 'The Osbournes'Thanks to The Osbournes making Kelly Osbourne famous, she went on to have the incredible experience of co-hosting The View for years. On top of that, starring in The Osbournes seemed to inspire Kelly to grow as a person and transform physically.For his part, Jack Osbourne also underwent a huge physical change and he seemed to be a lot more at peace with himself as he grew more mature. Sadly, however, Jack still had the misfortune of going through a divorce and the end of his marriage affected his family in a big way.

How Ozzy And Sharon Reacted To Jack Osbourne's Divorce

After Jack Osbourne and the actor Lisa Stelly became a couple in the early-2010s, their relationship moved quickly. In early 2012, the couple welcomed their first child into the world and then they tied the knot later that same year.

Related: Which Osbourne Kid Has The Highest Net Worth?

After Jack Osbourne and Lisa Stelly were wed in 2012, they remained married for seven years and had two more children. Able to suffer through the tragedy of a miscarriage together, for a long time it seemed like Jack and Lisa were destined to stay together for life.

Sadly, only a couple of months after their youngest child was born, Jack Osbourne announced that he and Lisa Stelly had separated in 2018. The following year Jack and Lisa finalized their divorce.

After Jack Osbourne and Lisa Stelly's divorce was finalized, Sharon Osbourne appeared on The Howard Stern Show. During the resulting interview, Sharon spoke about what led to the divorce and how bad she felt about the situation.

“I’m sad. I’m very, very sad. She’s a good girl. And the thing is it just didn’t work,” Osbourne continued. “They love each other, they can’t live with each other.”

Around the same time as her aforementioned Howard Sten interview, Sharon Osbourne spoke to People. During that conversation, Sharon revealed her rock star husband Ozzy Osbourne's emotional reaction to the news his son's marriage was ending.

“When Jack said that he was getting divorced, Ozzy cried. He said, ‘I’m crying for you because I know how painful it’s going to be, because I’ve been through it and I don’t want you to experience that pain.’”

On the bright side, Sharon also spoke about how she and Ozzy provided Jack's children with a sense of normalcy even as their parents' divorce forced a lot of changes.

“When this happens in families – and it happens all the time – they have to have the stability somewhere. Nana and Papa are still in the same house. Nothing’s going to change. You know what you’re going to get when you come here.”

How Jack Osbourne's Life Moved On After His Divorce

When people go through a life-changing process like divorce, it can be easy for them to feel lost for a long time. Of course, when children are involved that only serves to make things more emotional and difficult to deal with.

Related: Why Ozzy Osbourne's Son Has His Own Discovery + Reality Show, Night Of Terror

From all accounts, Jack Osbourne and Lisa Stelly's divorce was hard on them, their children, and their families. However, fortunately for Jack, he found someone new to share his life with.

The same year Jack Osbourne and Lisa Stelly's divorce was finalized, he began dating a woman named Aree Gearhart. Together ever since 2019, Jack and Aree got engaged after dating for roughly two years.

After Jack Osbourne popped the question to Aree Gearhart in 2021, the couple made a huge announcement in 2022. When Jack posted a photo of himself and his fiancee Aree on Instagram, it certainly looked like she was sporting a baby bump.

Once fans read the caption Jack Osbourne posted alongside that photo, it instantly became clear that the couple is expecting a baby. "Exciting news!!! @seecreature and I are expecting a baby in the summer! Baby #4 here we come!"

While it is clear that Jack Osbourne has gone through some tough times, including suffering from serious health issues, he has proved to be a survivor.
