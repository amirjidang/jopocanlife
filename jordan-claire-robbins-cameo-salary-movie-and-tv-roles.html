Does Jordan Claire Robbins Make More From Her Cameo Than Her Movie and TV Roles?

The Escape The Field star, who is also known for her role in the inspirational Netflix phenomenon, The Umbrella Academy Television series is not just an actress. The 33-year-old Bermudian-Canadian is also a producer, model, and writer, and she has worked in these diverse capacities over the course of her career.

Following her high school diploma in Bermuda, she moved to Toronto to specialize in Drama and Psychology at the University of Toronto. She debuted as an actress with the television series Man Seeking Woman, after which she played a recurrent role in the series 12 Monkeys. Both series were released in 2015. Robbins continued to appear in various films such as Anon and Fashionably Yours. She acted alongside Rose McIver in the TV show iZombie.

Since her breakthrough, Robbins has continued to make waves in the entertainment industry. She has made a name for herself through multiple appearances and diverse roles in numerous television shows and films.
