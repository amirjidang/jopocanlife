Josh Groban is an established musician, singer, actor, record producer and songwriter whose net worth is estimated to be around $30 million. Born in Los Angeles, he always had a flair for singing from early childhood. His fortune is said to be built with the help of his four platinum rated solo albums, some of which held multi-platinum records.

Initially, he was into acting but later focused his attention to singing, which he considers his real passion. When he was offered a recording contract, he went on to acquire formal training in singing. Eventually, he became associated with Producer David Foster who helped him in achieving a booming career. He used to work as a rehearsal artist with David as he had no prior experience in singing.

He impressed the show hostess of the event with his performance who later invited him to appear on her show's in the following weeks. He later tasted his success with "Awake", "Closer" and "Illuminations and all that echoes".
