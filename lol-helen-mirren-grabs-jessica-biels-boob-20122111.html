Helen Mirren Grabs Jessica Biel's Boob?

Jessica Biel‘s boobs are real, and they’re fantastic — and Dame Helen Mirren is impressed!

At the Tuesday Nov. 20 premiere of Hitchcock, Biel, 30, and Mirren, 67, caught up on the red carpet — where the Oscar-winning British actress marveled over Biel’s stunning black Gucci gown, which revealed more than a little side-boob.

As costar Toni Collette looked on, Mirren (herself resplendent in a black lace Dolce and Gabbana gown with gold details) moved in and placed her hand on the fabric of Biel’s dress — and in what appears to be an optical illusion, Mirren nearly cops a feel of Biel’s breast in one snapshot.  To her credit, Biel (who wed Justin Timberlake back in October) didn’t flinch.

Based on director Alfred Hitchcock‘s 1961 making of his classic film Psycho, the film stars Anthony Hopkins in the title role, Mirren as his wife, Alma, plus Scarlett Johansson (as Janet Leigh), Biel (as Vera Miles) and Collette (as Peggy Robertson).
