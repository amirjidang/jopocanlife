Deirdre Lovejoy net worth: How much is Deirdre Lovejoy worth?

Deirdre Lovejoy played Assistant State’s Attorney Rhonda Pearlman in HBO’s The Wire from 2002 to 2008. Then, from 2009 to 2011, she portrayed the recurring role of a serial kidnapper and killer Heather Taffet (also known as “The Gravedigger”) in the television show Bones.

Lovejoy had another appearance in the Cameron Diaz-starring movie Bad Teacher in 2011 as a student’s mother.

At Elkhart Memorial High School, Lovejoy graduated from high school in 1980. She earned her diploma from the University of Evansville in 1984 with a theater and communications major.

She graduated from the New York University Tisch School of the Arts with a Master of Fine Arts degree three years later.

She made her acting debut in 1990 in the ABC miniseries The Kennedys of Massachusetts as Rosemary Kennedy. Lovejoy had appearances on a variety of shows throughout the 1990s and 2000s, including Law & Order, Seinfeld, Third Watch, Spin City, NYPD Blue, The West Wing, Cold Case, and Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.
