Michael B. Jordan’s Love Life

Ever since Michael B. Jordan first rose to fame, there has been a perception that when compared to his Hollywood peers, he is a pretty private guy. Considering that a fair amount is known about Jordan’s love life over the years, however, that may not be the case. Instead, people may feel that way largely because he has avoided the kind of controversies that land most stars in the tabloid headlines.

When it comes to Michael B. Jordan’s love life, one of his earliest known relationships was with Australian rapper Iggy Azalea. After that relationship ended, Jordan dated Catherine Paiz from 2013 until 2014. Since 2017, Jordan has dated Saweetie, Ashlyn Castro, Kiki Layne, and Lori Harvey. It is also worth noting that there have been rumors of Jordan getting involved with other female stars but none of those pairings have been confirmed by anyone involved.

The Star Fans Wanted Michael B. Jordan Fans Want Him To Date

When Black Panther was released in 2018, moviegoers all over the world flocked to theaters to see the blockbuster film. Thankfully for everyone involved in the movie’s production, Marvel fans, and filmgoers everywhere, Black Panther turned out to be an almost universally acclaimed film that made a fortune at the box office.

Of course, there are several reasons why Black Panther was so successful including the popularity of the MCU, the movie’s amazing special effects, and its fantastic soundtrack. Despite all of that, it could easily be argued that the best part of Black Panther was its extremely talented cast. After all, everyone who starred in Black Panther was perfect for their role. As it turns out, however, many Black Panther fans wanted to see a couple of the film’s stars have a very different relationship in real life.

As Black Panther fans will no doubt remember, Michael B. Jordan and Lupita Nyong'o’s characters in the film were enemies who wanted to completely defeat each other. When the two actors have appeared in public together, however, they are absolutely adorable together. For example, when the world saw footage of Nyong'o making Jordan do push-ups in public after beating him in a bet, it was hard to not be charmed by their playful relationship. For that reason, many of Nyong'o and Jordan’s fans badly wanted the two actors to become a couple in public.

When looking at the history of Michael B. Jordan and Lori Harvey’s relationship, it quickly becomes clear that she hasn’t done anything to earn the ire of his fans.
