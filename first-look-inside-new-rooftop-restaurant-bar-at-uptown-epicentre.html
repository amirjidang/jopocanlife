First look inside new rooftop restaurant, bar at uptown EpiCentre WSOC TV

CHARLOTTE, N.C. — Nuvole Rooftop TwentyTwo is now welcoming guests to its perch atop the EpiCentre complex in uptown Charlotte.

That rooftop restaurant and bar is focused on tasty food, good drinks and creating an atmosphere to relax and gather, says Paul Picarazzi, principal, Vision Ventures.

[RELATED: 4 arrested, 13 guns seized from Epicenter parking deck, police say]

It sits on the 22nd floor of a new, dual-branded hotel over the EpiCentre. Last month, AC Hotel — the city’s first — and Residence Inn Charlotte City Center opened at 210 E. Trade St., adding 300 hotel rooms in uptown.

“The location lent itself to that, and it just didn’t exist. There was a void to fill,” Picarazzi says.
