Does Harry Hamlin's Forgotten Son Dimitri Get Along With His Half-Sisters Delilah Belle And Amelia G

Although Lisa Rinna and Harry Hamlin have welcomed cameras into their home on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, there are some things fans don't know about Lisa and Harry, and that includes Harry's son, Dimitri Hamlin, whom he shares with Ursula Andress.

Dimitri definitely doesn't get as much attention as his half-sisters Delilah Belle and Amelia Gray who are not only models with massive Instagram followings but also the daughters of two actors who were on reality TV for several years.

While fans haven't heard a lot about Dimitri Hamlin, is he close with his half-siblings Amelia Gray and Delilah Belle, and what does their relationship look like?

What Is Dimitri Hamlin's Relationship With Delilah Belle And Amelia Gray Hamlin Like?

Fans want to know all about Lisa Rinna's bond with her daughters, and while the entire family seems to be really close, do all of Harry Hamlin's children get along?

While Dimitri Hamlin hasn't spoken about his relationship with his half-sisters, and Delilah Belle and Amelia Gray Hamlin haven't said anything either, it seems that they are fairly close. Or at least close enough for Dimitri to celebrate the release of Delilah Belle's song "Nothing Lasts Forever."

Dimitri was at Delilah Belle's party in March 2023 at Hotel Ziggy, according to Yahoo!, and Delilah Belle shared in a caption on her Instagram account, “So incredibly grateful to everyone who has supported me on this journey. From all of my followers to my friends and family THANK YOU."

It's sweet that Delilah Belle invited her half-brother and wanted him there to celebrate this next phase of her career.

In interviews about her music and on her Instagram account, Delilah appears excited and content, and if she didn't get along with Dimitri, it seems that she wouldn't have invited him to the event.

Although Dimitri Hamlin has famous actors as parents, it doesn't appear that he wants to be as well-known as they are. He doesn't have any social media accounts and doesn't give interviews. It isn't clear how often Dimitri sees his father or his half-siblings, but there are photos of them at events from time to time, and they appear to get along.

While Ursula Andress had Dimitri in Rome, he lives in Los Angeles. According to The Los Angeles Times, Ursula had a house in Beverly Hills that she put on the market in 2017 and made $2.28 million. Since Dimitri lives in the same place as his half-siblings, that must make it super easy for them to see each other.

Who Is Harry Hamlin's Son Dimitri Hamlin?

According to People, Ursula Andress gave birth to Dimitri Hamlin in 1980 and she and Harry Hamlin dated until 1983. The publication reported that Lisa Rinna and Harry Hamlin started dating in 1992.

Lisa gave birth to their first child Delilah Belle in 1998 and in 2001, they welcomed Amelia Gray.

There is a big age difference between Dimitri Hamlin and his half-sisters: he is 42 years old, Delilah is 24, and Amelia is 21.

Ursula Andress starred in Dr. No, the inaugural James Bond movie that was released in 1962 as Honey Ryder, a Bond girl. She has starred in many movies like What's New Pussycat? and Double Murder, although her Bond role is absolutely the one that people think about when they hear her name.

Hollywood Life reported that Dimitri has been an actor, including starring on a show that aired in Italy called Caterina e le sue figlie (translated to My Daughters). He has also been a DJ, a model, and been in music videos.

According to his IMDb page, Dimtiri also starred in a short film called Say Cheese! in 2022.

Similar to his actor parents, Dimitri Hamlin has definitely worked in artistic fields throughout his life. After he went to Princeton and studied philosophy, he began writing for The Huffington Post.

His author page on the website shows that he wrote several articles that were published in 2011 and 2012 with titles like "Is That All There Is? It's About Time" and "Is That All There Is? Rugged Individualism." It seems that this column allowed him to think about concepts that he studied in philosophy classes.

What Has Lisa Rinna Said About Harry Hamlin's Son Dimitri Hamlin?

Lisa Rinna spoke about Dimitri Hamlin in an episode of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. According to People, she said, "He said she called him and said, 'Harry, come to my room.' And so he did, at 28 years old, he went in and she got pregnant that night. And that was that, at 44. First time."

Lisa also said that when Harry and Ursula were dating, they stayed at the home that she has shared with Harry for years. She said, "He was with Ursula Andress at the time and they had a child, she lived here. They were doing Clash of the Titans together."

Lisa Rinna and Harry Hamlin are very close with their children. Lisa Rinna talked about her husband and daughters in an interview with ModernMom in 2008.

According to People, she shared an adorable story about Harry playing with Amelia and Delilah, who were six and nine years old at the time. Lisa said, "The other day, the girls were playing hair salon, and Harry was the client. He had rubber bands in his hair – can you just picture that? He will sit there for hours. He is so patient. I learn from him."

Lisa also spoke about making sure to be there for her daughters despite her busy and bustling acting career.

She said, "I am in the moment. When I am with the children, I am WITH them. They know that and they feel that. When you’re with the kids, you should really pay attention — and not think about the grocery list or anything else. Or, when I am working, I am really doing that."

Considering how much time Lisa Rinna and Harry Hamlin spend with Amelia Gray and Delilah Belle, and the warm family life they have prioritized, it seems easy to assume that they are just as close with Dimitri Hamlin. When Lisa spoke about Dimitri on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, she sounded friendly and respectful of the fact that her husband had a child with someone else, and it sounds like she accepted it right away.
