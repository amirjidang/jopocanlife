Watch Trailer For Dark Side Of The Rings Brawl For All Documentary

After debuting last week with two episodes on Chris Benoit, VICE’s Dark Side of the Ring has announced its third episode, which will focus on the Brawl For All tournament that WWE hosted in 1998.

The episode will air on Tuesday, April 7, at 10:00pm ET/PT. Following the episode, a new weekly after-show hosted by comedian and wrestling fan Chris Gethard will air, and will feature a panel of guests from the worlds of wrestling and entertainment who talk about the episode.
