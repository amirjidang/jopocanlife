The Voice: A Ranking Of The Winners, According To Net Worth

Highlights

  • The Voice winners' net worths range from $100,000 to $4 million.
  • Several winners have found success in the music industry through album releases, touring, and collaborations.
  • Some winners have taken a break from music or pursued independent careers after their appearance on the show.

The Voice has been on the air since 2011 and has had 24 seasons to date. This means that there have been numerous singers to come off the stage as winners, but not all of them have found success. Some have risen to fame after this series, while others have had a harder time gaining a foothold in the industry.

This list seeks to rank the top ten winners based on their current net worth following their appearance on the show. A few of their earnings might surprise fans as some have taken their careers to the next level.

Keep reading to learn about the ranking of The Voice winners based on their net worth!

Updated December 2023: Michael Huntley, known professionally as Huntley, was the winner of Season 24 of 'The Voice'. This made coach, Niall Horan a two-time winner, having coached the winner of Season 23, Gina Miles.

22 Michael Huntley

Net Worth: $100,000

  • Winner of Season 24

Michael Huntley, who goes by the name Huntley professionally, is the most recent winner of The Voice. His win in December 2023 was done with the help of his coach, Niall Horan. A perfect send off for Horan, who will be touring in 2024.

Huntley has no plans right now to put out new music. Instead, he wants to "take a step back and breathe" after being away from his family for six months. This does not mean that Huntley is walking away from music. In fact, he cannot wait to get out and tour with new music he has written.

Michael Huntley's net worth is $100,000.

21 Gina Miles

Net Worth: $100,000

  • Winner of Season 23

Gina Miles became the champion of Season 23 of The Voice under the direction of coach Niall Horan.

While Miles has not officially released any new music via her record company, she did release a single she had been working on via Instagram in July 2023 called "Someone New".

Gina Miles has a net worth of $100,000.

Chloe Kohanski was the winner of Season 13 of The Voice. Her coach, Blake Shelton, gave Kohanski tips throughout the season, which paid off with a "W" for the two.

These days, Kohanski goes by Chloe MK professionally. And while she was signed by Republic Records, Kohanski has decided since 2020 to release music on her own.

19 Javier Colon

Net Worth: $200,000

  • Winner of Season 1

The first season of the show gave fans a winner named Javier Colon. His coach was Adam Levine. He has released two albums since his debut and even sells self-branded merchandise on his website for some extra cash. Colon is still doing concerts, but his career has slowed down as he hasn't released another album since 2016.

Javier Colon's net worth is $200,000.

18 Jake Hoot

Net Worth: $200,000

  • Winner of Season 17

Jake Hoot won Season 17 of The Voice with the help of coach, Kelly Clarkson. She was the only coach to turn their chair during the Blind Auditions. But that was all that was needed to get Hoot to the finale and ultimately win the season.

Since The Voice, Hoot has yet to release an album. However, he released the single, "I Would've Loved You" featuring Clarkson. The 2021 song release has over 660,000 streams on Spotify and over 1.5 million video views on YouTube.

Jake Hoot's net worth is $200,000.

16 Alisan Porter

Net Worth: $500,000

  • Winner of Season 10

Alisan Porter won Season 10 of The Voice with the assistance of her coach, Christina Aguilera.

The former child star, known for her role in Curly Sue, released her recent single, "Meant for Me" in December 2023. She plans on touring in 2024.

Alisan Porter's net worth is $500,000.

15 Carter Rubin

Net Worth: $500,000

  • Winner of Season 19

Carter Rubin was the youngest member of The Voice to date, being just 15 years old. He got this distinction by being coached by Gwen Stefani.

Rubin released a single, "Time Machine" in 2022. His recent single, "Mess Me Up", released in 2023, has been downloaded on Spotify over 20,000 times.

Rubin's net worth is $500,000.

14 Maelyn Jarmon

Net Worth: $500,000

  • Winner of Season 16

Maelyn Jarmon won Season 16 of The Voice with coach, John Legend's help. Since that time, she released a single, "Wait for You" on Spotify, which has over 900,000 streams.

Next for Jarmon is a starring role in the play, The Last Five Years. The play begins in February 2024.

Maelyn Jarmon's net worth is $500,000.

13 Chris Blue

Net Worth: $600,000

  • Winner of Season 12

Chris Blue was the winner from Season 12. A big part of his current routine has become songwriting, as he has now written over 100 songs with the help of his manager.

He debuted his first album back in 2019 called Fresh Start and does not currently have any tours scheduled, but he has continued to work on new music in the time being.

Chris Blue's net worth is $600,000.

12 Tessanne Chin

Net Worth: $1 Million

  • Winner of Season 5

The winner of Season 5 was a woman named Tessanne Chin. Tessanne has recently taken a step back from music as she welcomed her first child, but she is still very active on social media.

This singer has opened for several artists, as well as toured for three years with Jimmy Cliff after she released her debut album called Count on My Love back in 2014.

Tessanne Chin's net worth is $1 million.

11 Jermaine Paul

Net Worth: $1 Million

  • Winner of Season 2

Season 2 of The Voice brought a man named Jermaine Paul to the stage. He won the season with the help of his coach, Blake Shelton.

Paul originally served as a backup singer for Alicia Keys before his debut on the show and hasn't done much since he won back in 2012.

Paul has released quite a few singles, but his album which was supposed to be released back in 2015 still has yet to make an appearance.

Jermaine Paul's net worth is $1 million.

10 Josh Kaufman

Net Worth: $1 Million

  • Winner of Season 6

Josh Kaufman won Season 6 of The Voice. With the help of his coach, Usher, Kaufman made his way to the finale and won it all.

Kaufman played the role of Pippin on Broadway for a short time after winning The Voice.

Kaufman is still releasing music and his most recent album is called NDOXO Vol. 1 and many of the songs are written with his good friend Bleu McAuley.

Josh Kaufman net worth is $1 million.

9 Sundance Head

Net Worth: $1 Million

  • Winner of Season 11

Sundance Head won back in Season 11 with the help of his coach, Blake Shelton.

A part of his struggles stems from his contract with Republic Records following the show, as they had no need for a country artist.

This didn't stop him though as he found a way out of his contract as he has now released one album called Stained Glass and Neon since his time on the show. He also has plenty of upcoming tour dates and has been working to play at more venues to advance his career.

Sundance Head's net worth is $1 million.

8 Girl Named Tom

Net Worth: $1 Million

  • Winner of Season 21

Girl Named Tom is made up of family members, Bekah Liechty, Joshua Liechty, and Caleb Liechty. They competed on The Voice under Kelly Clarkson's team.

The group continues to tour the Midwest today. They have released one album, One More Christmas which they used for a Christmas tour in 2022.

Girl Named Tom is worth $1 million.

7 Brynn Cartelli

Net Worth: $1.5 Million

  • Winner of Season 14

Brynn Cartelli was the winner of Season 14 of The Voice. She was coached by Kelly Clarkson, who had her first with Cartelli.

Since the win, Cartelli signed with Elektra Records, opened for Clarkson's Meaning of Life Tour, and released her most recent single, "Secondhand Smoke" in 2023. Cartelli even directed the music video for the single.

Brynn Cartelli has a net worth of $1.5 million.

6 Sawyer Fredericks

Net Worth: $1.5 Million

  • Winner of Season 8

Sawyer Fredericks won Season 8 of The Voice. He has released several albums since he won the show at just 15 years old.

Fredericks is constantly on tour at smaller venues where fans can hear his unique sound and appreciate all this artist has to offer.

Sawyer Fredericks' net worth is $1.5 million.

5 Bryce Leatherwood

Net Worth: $1.5 Million

  • Winner of Season 22

Bryce Leatherwood was the winner of Season 22 of The Voice. His coach, Blake Shelton announced that Season 22 would be his last, and Leatherwood allowed Shelton to go out on top.

Since The Voice, Leatherwood has signed with Republic Records/Universal Music Group Nashville and will be headed out on a five-month tour starting in 2024.

Leatherwood's net worth is $1.5 million.

4 Jordan Smith

Net Worth: $2 Million

  • Winner of Season 9

Season 9 of The Voice gave the world a singer named Jordan Smith. With the help of his coach, Adam Levine, Smith took home the grand prize.

Smith has released three albums since his debut, and one of them was a specialty album filled with covers of famous Christmas songs.

The music he has created has been insanely popular and fans expect him to announce a new album soon as it has been a few years since his last.

Jordan Smith's net worth is $2 million.

3 Craig Wayne Boyd

Net Worth: $2.3 Million

  • Winner of Season 7

Craig Wayne Boyd was the winner of Season 7 of The Voice. His coach was Blake Shelton.

This artist released his first album back in 2017 called Top Shelf and has more recently released an EP called From the InBetween. He also has a podcast where his fans can go to listen to him speak on various topics in an effort to learn more about this artist.

Craig Wayne Boyd has a net worth of $2.3 million.
