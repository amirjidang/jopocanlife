The Cast Of Netflix's Outer Banks May Not Be Making Nearly As Much As You'd Think

The Outer Banks is a chain of barrier islands off the coast of North Carolina. The area is known for its beautiful beaches, lush vegetation, and mild climate. It's no wonder that the Netflix show Outer Banks, which is set on these islands, has been so popular since it was released in April 2020. Viewers are drawn to the show for its thrilling plot, which follows a group of teens who get wrapped up in a treasure hunt. The characters are relatable and the setting is stunning. It's no surprise that Outer Banks is one of the top 10 most-watched shows on Netflix.

Being that the cast of the show is made up of relatively new actors, they aren’t worth as much per episode right off the bat. When the show grows in popularity and is in higher demand, the actors start getting paid a little more to keep them around. The cast of Stranger Things is a great example of this, they went from may-day to payday in four short seasons. However, if you’re thinking the Outer Banks cast is similar in comparison, you should probably think again; there is quite a big difference. Below we have what the main cast (minus the three new members) makes per episode, and we think you’ll be as surprised as we were.

7 How Much Does Madelyn Cline Of Outer Banks Make Per Episode?

In the first season of Outer Banks, breakout star Madelyn Cline plays the role of Sarah Cameron, one of the main characters in the show. Cline's salary for the first season is rumored to be around $30,000 per episode, which puts her total earnings for the 10-episode season at approximately $300,000. This makes Cline one of the highest-paid actors on the show.

Cline got a raise for the second season of Outer Banks, which is really what got fans hooked on this teen drama, and it has been said that her salary increased to $40,000 per episode for the second season. This would put her total earnings for the season at $400,000.

As for her third season, Madelyn Cline had stepped it up big time. She is currently one the biggest up-and-coming stars in Hollywood and even took a role in another Netflix series, Glass Onion : A Knives Out Mystery, in between seasons of Outer Banks. She reportedly made upwards of $80,000 per episode in season three. That would give her a $800,000 payday, which tracks with her said net worth.

6 How Much Does Chase Stokes Of Outer Banks Make Per Episode?

Chase Stokes, who originally turned down the role, plays John B on Netflix's Outer Banks.It is rumored that Stokes was originally the highest-paid actor on the show. Making $40,000 per episode when they first got started. He made around $800,000 for the third season, though, making him even with Madelyn Cline.

While $800,000 per season may seem like a lot of money, it is important to remember what these actors have bills to pay and a lot of people who work for them doing various behind-the-scenes things. Still, a major step up from a guy who was living in his car when he tried out for the role. Netflix originally planned to go four to five seasons with Outer Banks, so it’s highly likely we will be seeing it renewed for season four. This means that Stokes' salary will likely increase in the near future.

5 How Much Does Rudy Pankow Of Outer Banks Make Per Episode?

Rudy Pankow had appeared in a few short films and in some random episodes of TV shows prior to landing the role of JJ on Outer Banks. Like the roles of many of his cast members, this became his breakthrough performance. Pankow also discovered love on the set of the popular Netflix series.

He is presently dating an assistant that works behind-the-scenes on Outer Banks too, Elaine Siemek. It’s reported that Rudy made $30,000 per episode the first season and may likely make upwards of $50,000 now in the third season. Since Netflix has not officially released the pay of the actors for Outer Banks, these figures were all balanced against their net worth and what similar shows paid.

4 How Much Does Madison Bailey Of Outer Banks Make Per Episode?

Madison Bailey is a breakout star of the Netflix show Outer Banks. It is rumored that she made $30,000 per episode, which puts her total earnings for the first season at $300,000. For the second season, her salary was said to be increased to $40,000 per episode, which would put her total earnings for the season at $400,000. Like the other actors, Madison likely got a pay increase for the third season. Bailey also joined the American Horror Story franchise while Outer Banks wasn't filming.

3 How Much Does Jonathon Daviss Of Outer Banks Make Per Episode?

Jonathan Daviss, 23, grew up in the south even though he is not from North Carolina like his Outer Banks character. The actor grew up in Conroe, Texas, where he participated in high school football. He relocated to California with his family after graduating to pursue an acting career.

After appearing in movies like Edge of the World and Deliverance Creek, Daviss landed his breakthrough role as Pope in Outer Banks. Daviss has continued to work on Netflix films like many of his co-stars, most recently appearing in Do Revenge with Camila Mendes and Maya Hawke.

Jonathan Daviss is reported to have made $20,000 per episode when the show first began, and it has since increased to $50,000 per episode in season three.

2 How Much Does Austin North Of Outer Banks Make Per Episode?

Topper is the kooks leader. The rich boys on the island; kooks. In addition to being athletic, is incredibly wealthy and believes himself to be a member of an elite society, frequently coming across as better than everyone. It's because of this that The Pogues hate him. He has continually attacked them and displays anger toward them constantly.

Austin North started off on the Disney channel. His breakthrough role really was Outer Banks, as with the other young actors. He is said to have made $30,000 per episode in season one. As for season three, it’s figured that he is making $50,000 per episode.

1 How Much Does Charles Esten Of Outer Banks Make Per Episode?

Charles Esten is a well-known actor, singer, and musician who has been a series regular on Outer Banks since its debut in 2020. According to reports, Esten is being paid $80,000 per episode of Outer Banks.

Charles plays Ward on the show. Ward is Sarah’s father. In addition to his role on Outer Banks, Esten has also had roles in other TV shows and movies, such as Nashville and The Good Wife. His net worth is estimated to be $5 million.
